It has not been shown to the satisfaction of the Court that the Protective Policing Manual, the respondent intelligence file and the File No. P.O.B.-200 are necessary with respect to this finding of fact. The materials do not relate to a material fact in issue and the information is not relied upon by the R.C.M.P. as part of its defence. Accordingly, the impugned documents are not critical to Mr. Rankin's complaint against the R.C.M.P. and he would not be prejudiced by their nondisclosure.
