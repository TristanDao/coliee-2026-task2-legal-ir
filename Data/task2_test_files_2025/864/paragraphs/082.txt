This section seems to indicate early that the Chairman, not the Commission, has an obligation. He or she "shall review every complaint referred to the Commission pursuant to subs. 45.41(1)" at least so far as to conclude that disposition of the complaint by the R.C.M.P. is satisfactory, or unsatisfactory. If the latter is the case the Chairman may then request or undertake further steps in relation to the complaint.
