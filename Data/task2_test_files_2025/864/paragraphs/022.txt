It may be trite to record that questions of retrospective application of the statute arise only in relation to conduct giving rise to complaints, which conduct is alleged to have occurred before proclamation of relevant Parts of the Act or before Royal Assent. There is no question of retrospective application of the legislation to complaints sought to be presented or referred to the Commission before September 30, 1988 when Part VII came into force. In the latter sense the legislation operates only prospectively.
