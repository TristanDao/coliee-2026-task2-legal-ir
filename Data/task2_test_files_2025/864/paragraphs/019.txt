All of the complaints outlined with the stated case as examples, except Complaint "C-2", were initiated by presentation to the R.C.M.P., not to the Commission, before Part VII of the Act was proclaimed and these were dealt with in accord with the R.C.M.P. complaints procedures. As noted in the summary table above all were "disposed of", i.e., investigated, if appropriate, or otherwise dealt with and reported on in accord with those procedures.
