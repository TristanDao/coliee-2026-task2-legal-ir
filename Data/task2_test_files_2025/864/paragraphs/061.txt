The issue to be determined under this heading is simply, it would appear, "how far back" the retrospective application of Parts VI and VII of the Act may reach. The stated case asks whether the Commission has jurisdiction pursuant to Part VII of the Act to entertain complaints arising from conduct by the R.C.M.P. alleged to have occurred:
a) between proclamation of Part VI (December 18, 1986) and proclamation of Part VII (September 30, 1988) (Category "A" complaints);
b) between March 26, 1986 (Royal Assent) and proclamation of Part VI (December 18, 1986) (Category "B" complaints); and
c) prior to Royal Assent (March 26, 1986) (Complaints "C-1" and "C-2").
