Certain sections of Part VII of the Act impose upon the Commission or its Chairman mandatory duties. With respect to complaints initially to the Commission under s. 45.35(1)(a), these must be acknowledged (s. 45.35(2)) and the Commissioner of the R.C.M.P. must be notified of every complaint so initiated (s. 45.35(3)). The Chairman has an obligation under s. 45.37 where he initiates a complaint, and where a complaint is referred to the Commission under s. 45.41, to notify the Commissioner.
