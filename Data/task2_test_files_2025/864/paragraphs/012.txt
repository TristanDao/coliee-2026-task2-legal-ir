The Attorney General submits that the Commission has no jurisdiction to deal with complaints involving conduct which occurred prior to September 30, 1988, when Part VII was proclaimed in force. Alternatively, if the court determines that the amending Act is to have retrospective effect, applying to complaints about conduct alleged to have occurred before that date, that retrospectivity ought not to extend to complaints about conduct which occurred prior to March 26, 1986 when the amending Act received Royal Assent.
