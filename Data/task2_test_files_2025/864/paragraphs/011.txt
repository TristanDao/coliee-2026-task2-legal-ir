It is argued by the Attorney General of Canada that the jurisdiction of the Commission to entertain the Category "A", "B" and "C" complaints is not supported by the legislation, since a decision by the Commission to consider any complaints of this nature would require that Part VII of the Act be applied retrospectively in relation to the conduct or events giving rise to the complaints. The extent of the retrospective application, it is pointed out, would vary for each of the three categories. The Act does not expressly provide for the retrospective application of Part VII.
