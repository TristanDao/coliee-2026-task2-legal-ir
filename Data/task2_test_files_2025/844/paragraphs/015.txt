Counsel for the defendants suggests that rule 1733 is not applicable since I have not had to deal with a matter arising subsequent to a judgment; with this I agree. In conclusion, he argued that if I render such an order it would be premature since the assessment of damages has not yet been concluded and, any attempt to rely on rule 1900 by condemning the bail bond, would in fact be levying execution. I disagree. The order sought will not be contrary to rule 1900 since I would not be ordering levy or execution but only require that monies due under the bonds be paid into court.
