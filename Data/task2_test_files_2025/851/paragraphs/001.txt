[1] Gibson, J.
: This is an application for judicial review of a decision of the Convention Refugee Determination Division (the "CRDD") of the Immigration and Refugee Board dated the 9th day of June, 1993, wherein the CRDD determined the applicant not to be a Convention refugee within the meaning of the
Immigration Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. I-2, by reason of the fact that the applicant is a person to whom the United Nations
Convention Relating to the Status of Refugees
(the "
Convention
") does not apply pursuant to section F of Article 1 of the
Convention
. Section F of Article 1 of the
Convention
is set out in the schedule to the
Immigration Act
and reads as follows:
"F. The provisions of this Convention shall not apply to any person with respect to whom there are serious reasons for considering that:
(a) he has committed a crime against peace, a war crime, or a crime against humanity, as defined in the international instruments drawn up to make provision in respect of such crimes;
(b) he has committed a serious non-political crime outside the country of refuge prior to his admission to that country as a refugee;
(c) he has been guilty of acts contrary to the purposes and principles of the United Nations."
