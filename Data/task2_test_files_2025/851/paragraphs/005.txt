The applicant fled Sri Lanka and came to Canada in 1984. He was deemed to be a Convention refugee by the relevant Minister of the Government of Canada in February of 1985. He obtained landed status in Canada on the 26th day of November, 1986. On the 30th day of August, 1990, he was convicted of conspiracy to traffic in a narcotic and sentenced to three years' imprisonment. As a result, a report was prepared against him under s. 27 of the
Immigration Act
. At his deportation hearing, a conditional deportation order was issued. He once again claimed Convention refugee status.
