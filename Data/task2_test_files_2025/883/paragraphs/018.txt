Under s. 18.1 of the
Federal Court Act
, an application for judicial review must be made in respect of a decision or order of a federal board, commission or other tribunal. Rule 1602(4) provides that the notice of motion shall be in respect of a single decision, order or matter only. It is the decision by the Commissioner to issue notices under s. 13 of the
Inquiries Act
which is sought to be reviewed.
