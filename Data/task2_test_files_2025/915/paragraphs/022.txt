As I raised my concerns in turn with the Plaintiff's counsel, the Morris Defendant was of no assistance in their resolution. In
International Taekwon-Do Federation v. Choi
, [2008] F.C.J. No. 1402 (A.O.), I considered the allowability of certain items in the absence of a full canvass as here of their associated issues. I indicated to the Plaintiff's counsel that some might argue that the claim here under counsel fee item 12 (notice to admit facts or notice for production) for the Statement of Agreed Facts should be disallowed because such work is already subsumed in fee item 13 (preparation for trial). Counsel argued that it was an essential document and involved work common to the sort of work under fee item 12. I accept that submission in these limited circumstances and allow it as presented.
