I am not the "Court" as that term is used in Rule 420(2): see
Sander Holdings Ltd. v. Canada (Minister of Agriculture)
, [2009] F.C.J. No. 720 (A.O.) and
Marshall v. Canada
, [2006] F.C.J. No. 1282 (A.O.). Therefore, I cannot exercise the otherwise jurisdiction of the Court in Rule 420(2) to negate or vary its application.
