Section 3 of the
Export and Import Permits Act
provides that the Governor-in-Council may establish an Export Control List and include thereon any article that the Governor-in-Council deems it necessary to control for certain listed purposes [see footnote 3]. One of the purposes for which the Governor-in-Council may place a product on the list, and that used in this case, is "to ensure that there is an adequate supply and distribution of the article in Canada for defence or other needs" [see footnote 4].
