With regard to the interpretation problems raised by the applicant at the interview, the Court observes that the applicant was represented by counsel and that it was not until one hour after the interview began that she raised this issue. The interview was held in French. The tribunal record indicates that the applicant signed documents in French and, in the notes at the port of entry, she had indicated that she could speak French and Somalian. The Court does not hesitate to find that there was no breach of procedural fairness.
