In any event, I am satisfied that s. 19 of the
Federal Court Act
together with the
Federal Courts Jurisdiction Act
of Manitoba confers jurisdiction on this court to entertain the intended third-party proceedings. Section 19 is part of a cooperative scheme under which the provinces may enact legislation conferring jurisdiction on the Federal Court to provide a forum for the resolution of all types of controversies. It is a unique procedural provision permitting intergovernmental disputes to be adjudicated in the Federal Court. A prerequisite to its operation, which has been met in the present case, is that the Legislature of the province involved has passed legislation conferring jurisdiction on the Federal Court or Exchequer Court.
