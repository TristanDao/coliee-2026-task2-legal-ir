Zinn, J.
: This is an application for judicial review, pursuant to s. 72 of the
Immigration and Refugee Protection Act
, R.S.C. 2001, c. 27, of a decision by the Refugee Protection Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board. The Board determined that Chao Hui Lin was neither a Convention refugee nor a person in need of protection within the meaning of the Act. The determinative issue was the objective basis for the applicant's fear of persecution.
