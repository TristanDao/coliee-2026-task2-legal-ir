In addition, the Board made the following specific findings related to the applicant practising his faith in Fujian:
1. His underground church had not been raided by the PSB;
2. The only mention in the documentary evidence of arrests of Christians in Fujian was in 2002, when 20 Christians were arrested; and
3. The applicant's church was small, consisting of nine members, with no regular participation of a pastor and thus had none of the characteristics found in churches targeted by the state.
