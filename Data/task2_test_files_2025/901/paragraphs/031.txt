I reject this submission entirely. I accept the submission of the respondent that when, on this form, a main box is checked, such as Credibility, but none of the subordinate boxes under that heading are checked, it puts the applicant on notice that all of the issues in the unchecked subordinate boxes are at issue. It is only when some of the subordinate boxes are checked and some not that the applicant is alerted that those unchecked under the heading are not at issue.
