The applicant here submits that given the Board's finding that he was a member of an underground church and a Christian, the Board had a duty to examine and determine whether he would be persecuted from practicing his religion if returned. He relies on my decision in
Zhu v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, 2008 FC 1066 at para. 13. It is submitted that no such analysis was undertaken by the Board.
