The applicant raises a number of issues with respect to the Board's decision, which I would describe as follows:
1. Whether the Board erred in failing to consider what would happen to the applicant if he were to return to China;
2. Whether the Board failed to address the applicant's evidence of the raid on his church;
3. Whether the Board erred in its analysis of the documentary evidence; and
4. Whether the Board breached the applicant's right to procedural fairness in failing to alert him on the screening form that an issue before it was the consistency between his allegations and the documentary evidence.
ANALYSIS
Whether the Board erred in failing to consider what would happen to the applicant
if he were to return to China.
