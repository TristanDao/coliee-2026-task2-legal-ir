Clearly the Board erred; however, the Court must ask whether this error is of such a magnitude that its decision might have been different if it had not been made. I am of the view that the Board's determination on the merits would have remained unchanged. The evidence is of
one
arrest of the entire population of Fujian Province. Further it appears to have been an arrest related to a crack-down preceding the Olympics. It is, at best,
de minimus
evidence and, in my assessment, more would have been required to show that the decision would have differed had the error not been made.
