Hughes, J.
: The Applicant Mohammad Farid Khan, himself a widower, married his deceased brother’s wife in Pakistan. He is a Pakistani by origin and now resides in Canada, his new wife is Pakistani and presently resides there. The Applicant sought to bring his new wife to Canada but her application for a permanent resident visa was refused by the visa officer. An appeal from that decision to the Immigration and Refugee Board was dismissed. This Court is asked to judicially review that decision of the Board.
