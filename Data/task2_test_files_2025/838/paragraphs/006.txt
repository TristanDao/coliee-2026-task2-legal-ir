On December 10, 2007, the Board concluded that the applicants are not Convention refugees or persons in need of protection. In reaching its decision, the Board accepted, on the basis of the evidence proffered, that the applicants were beaten and robbed by Afro-Guyanese thieves in May 2006. In this regard, the Board stated at page 3 of its decision:
The panel accepts that the claimants were unfortunately robbed and beaten in May 2006. This was substantiated by the police and medical reports by the claimants.
