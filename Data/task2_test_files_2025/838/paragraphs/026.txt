The applicants argue that this Court should send the matter back to the Board with a direction that the Board find the applicants to be Convention refugees. I do not agree. It is not for this Court to decide whether effective state protection is available in Guyana, but rather to review the Board's decision to determine whether it was reasonable. Having concluded that that decision was not reasonable, this matter must be returned to a differently constituted panel for redetermination.
