On April 1, 1999, Parks Canada became a separate agency as that term is used in s. 2 of the
Public Service Labour Relations Act
, S.C. 2003, c. 22 ("
PSLRA
"), and as identified in Schedule V of the
Financial Administration Act
, R.S., c. F-10 ("
FAA
"). As a result, the employer for employees of Parks Canada was no longer Treasury Board, but Parks Canada itself.
