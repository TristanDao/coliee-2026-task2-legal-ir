Turning now to the merit of this application, a few preliminary remarks are in order. First of all, I agree with the respondent that during the period of April 1, 1997 to March 31, 2005, Parks Canada had no authority or responsibility for setting the terms and conditions of employment for the applicant, including classification and the content of job description. During that period, such authority rested with Treasury Board, as represented by PWGSC. This point was confirmed in the two Memoranda of Agreement between PWGSC and Parks Canada, which brought the applicant to Parks Canada on assignment and to which I have already referred at para. 3 of these reasons. As a result, any grievance the applicant may have for any period of time prior to his becoming an employee of Parks Canada is with the Treasury Board.
