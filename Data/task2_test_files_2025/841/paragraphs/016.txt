Mr. Peck argues essentially that he has not been treated fairly, because he did not receive the PM-06 classification despite the fact that he demonstrated the ability to do the job through 10 years of superior performance, and because he has received the same classification as the other 22 Asset Managers even though his job had a higher degree of responsibility and complexity. Accordingly, the issue to be decided on this application for judicial review is whether the decision made by Mr. Latreille is reviewable, on the basis of the appropriate standard of judicial review to be applied to such a decision.
