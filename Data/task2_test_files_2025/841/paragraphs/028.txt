Quite apart from the Memoranda of Agreement which set out the respective responsibilities of PWGSC and Parks Canada, the latter is clearly a separate entity since April 1, 1999. The
PSLRA
defines Parks Canada as a separate employer from Treasury Board (see
PSLRA
, s. 2, def. of "employer", and Schedule V of the
FAA
). Likewise, the
Parks Canada Agency Act
, S.C. 1998, c. 31 ("
PCAA
") states that ss. 11.1(1) and 12(2) of the
FAA
, which sets out the powers of the Treasury Board and of the deputy heads with respect to human resources management, do not apply to the Agency (
PCAA
, s. 13(3)). The
Act
specifically grants the Agency exclusive authority to act as employer independent of the Treasury Board.
