The Applicant, a citizen of Libya, came to Canada with her husband, on January 1, 1999, as a visitor. On January 16, 1999, the Applicant and her husband claimed protection as Convention refugees. The Immigration and Refugee Board, Convention Refugee Determination Division rejected her husband's claim on the basis that he was excluded from Convention refugee status pursuant to Article 1(F)(a) of the United Nations
Convention Relating to the Status of Refugees
, Can T.S. 1969 No. 6. An application for leave and judicial review was dismissed.
