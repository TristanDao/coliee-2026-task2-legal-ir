The member noted the fragile mental health of Mr. Cartagena, but maintained his finding of the existence of a viable IFA despite the psychological opinion in evidence. Psychological evidence is central to the question of whether the IFA is reasonable and cannot be disregarded:
Singh v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
