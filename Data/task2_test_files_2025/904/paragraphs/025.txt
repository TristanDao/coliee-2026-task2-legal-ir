"Audi alteram partem" is at the heart of natural justice. It means that Mr. Sahakyan had the right to be heard, to know the case he had to meet and to have the opportunity to respond to that case. The officer had a legitimate concern in his late departure. Even though Citizenship and Immigration, Montreal, had informed him it had no objection to Mr. Sahakyan's returning to Canada, the decision was his to make. He would have been derelict in his duty if he ignored the late departure. However, his duty was to enquire, a duty he did not discharge.
