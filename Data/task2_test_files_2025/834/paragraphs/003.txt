He entered Canada from the USA in January 2003. He made a refugee claim the next month, which was denied on February 17, 2004. The claim was denied because the Applicant misrepresented himself, his PIF did not mention that he had been in the USA for 3 years before coming to Canada. The Board found that he was not credible and that he had an Internal Flight Alternative. Judicial review was sought, but leave was denied.
