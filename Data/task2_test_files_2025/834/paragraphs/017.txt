On judicial review of a decision on an H & C application, the standard of review is reasonableness simpliciter (Baker v MCI, [1999] 2 S.C.R. 817). Further, the Court of appeal stated that: "It is not the role of the courts to re-examine [page369] the weight given to the different factors by the officers". (Canada (MCI) v Legault, [2002 4 F.C. 358 FCA, par. 11)
