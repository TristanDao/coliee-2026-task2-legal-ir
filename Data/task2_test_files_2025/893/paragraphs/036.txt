Dear Madam/Sir:
Please be advised that I continue to represent Mr. Hassaballa in his application for PRRA consideration.
I am writing to enclose a document which just came to my attention today and which I believe is relevant to the case. I ask that you please consider this document in coming to a conclusion on Mr. Hassaballa’s application.
You will note that the publication date of the thesis is 1996; however,
I bring to your attention that this document was a private Master’s Thesis until it was published on the internet on April 15, 2005
. That is why it was completely impossible to have known about this document and to have provided it at the time of his refugee hearing. It is not reasonable to have expected the client or myself to have identified and submitted this document at an earlier date.
