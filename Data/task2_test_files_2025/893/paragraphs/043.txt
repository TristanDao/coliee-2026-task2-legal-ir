Furthermore, the officer’s reliance on “uncommon sources” does not justify this Court’s intervention either. The reference to NDTV.com concerns an address by President Mubarak at a university where he “exhorted Muslim scholars to teach tolerance and shun extremism”. The Asia Times source was relied on to demonstrate that the government investigates and arrests extremists, including members of the Gamaa Islamiya, a fact that was also reported in the 2003 U.S. DOS report filed by the applicant. Finally, the United Nations Cyber School Bus website provided general information about Cairo and the size of its population, expected to reach 13.2 million residents by 2010, which is in no way controversial information.
