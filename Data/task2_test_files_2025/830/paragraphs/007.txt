The applicant submits that the RPD member was perverse in drawing a negative inference from his failure to provide evidence from his parents and wife in spite of his testimony that he felt that objective evidence would carry more weight. He noted that the member did not attack the credibility of any of his evidence. He also noted several points at which he claimed that the member mischaracterized the evidence.
