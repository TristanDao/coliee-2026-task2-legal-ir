In an oral decision at the close of the hearing, the Refugee Protection Division (RPD) member found several points of the applicant's story implausible and thus dismissed his claim. The Panel noted the lack of evidence from his parents or wife. The Panel also held that Mr. Diazgranados could not adequately explain how the incident which he claims caused him to seek refugee protection was different from those previous, which had not caused him to flee Columbia. He had reavailed himself of Columbia's protection after a prolonged stay in Peru. The Panel also found it implausible that the applicant would not apply for visas for his wife and child to go to Canada with him if he believed that they were at risk.
