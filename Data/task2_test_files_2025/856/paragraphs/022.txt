The respondent also submitted that there was no prior use of the trademark in Canada by CGA Ltd. Section 16 entitles an applicant to registration of a trademark if, prior to the date of his use or his making known of it, no other person used or made known a confusing mark. It is clear from the evidence that the first use of the trademark by the respondent was when they received their first shipment of Ortaniques from the applicant, on or about March 7, 1978. On the basis of Manhattan Industries, supra, this constitutes "use" by the owner, not by Branson.
