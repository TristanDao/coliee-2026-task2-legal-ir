In February, 1978, Jetco, as agent for CGA Ltd., entered into a verbal agreement with Branson to supply Ortaniques to Canada. A shipment was made in 1978 on fixed terms, and further shipments continued in 1979 and 1981 on consignment. Jetco has made no shipments of the product through Branson since 1981. The evidence indicates that this was as a result of "deregulation" in Jamaica of the sale of Ortaniques, whereby the respondent was able to buy them directly from producers rather than solely through CGA Ltd. or Jetco. Mr. Hernal Hamilton, Director/General Manager of Jetco, testified that they sold no Ortaniques in Canada between 1981 and 1985 because they could not obtain a viable price, although Branson continued to import on their own from various producers.
