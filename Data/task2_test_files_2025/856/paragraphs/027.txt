The final submission by the respondent is that the applicant's right to expungement is prescribed by s. 17(2) of the Act; more than five years having elapsed since the registration date of March 20, 1980. The respondent, although it was the first user as well as the party "making known" the mark, did so in law on behalf of the rightful owner, whose consent it had not obtained. Branson therefore cannot invoke the limitation period in s. 17.
