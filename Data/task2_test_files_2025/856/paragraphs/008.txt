CGA Ltd. and Jetco claim they did not consent to or authorize Branson's registration of the trademark, and that at all times Branson was acting as agent for Jetco and through them for CGA Ltd. They therefore claim that Branson was under a legal obligation as agent and as fiduciary to register the trademark only on behalf of CGA Ltd., who they claim is the proper owner of the mark, and the only one entitled to registration.
