I wish to comment further that I have grave doubts as to the registrability of "Ortanique" as a trademark; it appears descriptive of a fruit whose existence was brought about by combining the orange and the tangerine, said to have a unique flavour. I presume from the evidence that "Ortanique" is the name by which this fruit is generally known, and is not exclusive to any one supplier or grower. However, no arguments were specifically directed at this issue.
