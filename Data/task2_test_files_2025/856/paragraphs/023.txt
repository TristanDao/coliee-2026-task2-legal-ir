In this respect I quote the editorial note to Manhattan Industries, supra, as quoted by Reed, J., in Waxoyl, supra, at p. 122:
"Many internationally known marks are sold through distributors in Canada. The mark is still that of the original supplier, not the distributor.
.....
"The question is
not who is using
the mark but
whose mark is being used
. As long as the wares originate from the owner it is suggested that his mark is being used even if no sales in Canada are directly made by that owner." (emphasis added)
