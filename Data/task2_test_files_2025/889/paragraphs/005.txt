The couple started to receive threatening letters. The letters said that the couple must leave the city or their house would be burned down, with them inside; that they deserved death. The couple took the letters to the police. They were told that the police would look for the authors but that the letters were only threats and not crimes. The applicants believe that the police knew well the authors of these letters, sympathized with them, and did not wish to help the claimants.
