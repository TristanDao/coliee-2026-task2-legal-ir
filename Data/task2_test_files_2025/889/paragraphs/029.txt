"In most cases the inability to protect against random terrorist attacks will not constitute an inability of the state to protect. But
Ward

Mendivil

In the present case there is a specifically and directly targeted applicant who is a member of a small targeted group. This distinguishes the case from most cases which involve random incidents of terrorism."
