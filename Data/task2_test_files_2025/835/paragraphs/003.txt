Throughout the spring and fall of 1996, the Applicant engaged in negotiations about the sale of certain assets of CCG. It entered into a confidentiality agreement with the Government concerning information acquired in the course of investigating the operations of CCG, as part of the bidding process. It was agreed that any analyses, compilations, forecasts, studies or other documents prepared in respect of this investigation would be kept confidential "in perpetuity".
