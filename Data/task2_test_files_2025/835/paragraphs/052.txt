A confidentiality provision in an agreement for the production and television broadcast of the Canada Day shows for 1996 and 1997 was considered by Teitelbaum, J., in
Canadian Broadcasting Corp. v. National Capital Commission
(1998), 147 F.T.R. 264 (T.D.). He concluded that a confidentiality agreement may be imposed in relation to the parties to that agreement but it cannot affect the access rights of a third party making a request under the
Act
