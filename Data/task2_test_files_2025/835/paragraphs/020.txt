The Applicant argues that the Requested Records should be protected under section 20(1)(d) of the
Act
since their disclosure could reasonably be expected to interfere with the contractual or other negotiations of a third party. It says that the Agreement reveals its negotiation strategy. Such disclosure would interfere with its ability to negotiate and enter into agreements, on the most favourable terms, to purchase provincial government printing businesses that may come on the market through future privatization processes.
