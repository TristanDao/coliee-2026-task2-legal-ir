There is conflicting authority as to the effect to be given to a confidentiality agreement such as the one in issue here. In
Keddy v. Atlantic Canada Opportunities Agency
(1993), 66 F.T.R. 227; 50 C.P.R.(4th) 484 (T.D.) at 490-491, it was held that where the Government gives an undertaking of confidentiality, that undertaking constitutes evidence that the information in question is confidential and is a factor for requiring the Government to refuse to disclose a record under section 20(1)(b) of the
