The applicant submits that it was unreasonable for the panel to ignore relevant facts in denying his PFV, i.e. that he had successfully participated in PFVs between 1994 and 1997, and completed sex offender programmes in the past including the Workworth sex offender programme in 1992 and 1995 as well as the Long Termer's sex offender programme at Kingston in 2002. Further, the applicant submits that the panel ignored the fact that there was no sexual component to his present offences.
