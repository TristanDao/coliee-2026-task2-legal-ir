Concerning the first question, this Court determined in
Vaval et al. v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, [2007] F.T.R. Uned. 126; [2007] F.C.J. No. 227; 2007 FC 160, at para. 7, that "... The existence of a nexus between the alleged persecution and one of the five grounds listed in the definition of 'Convention refugee' under section 96 of the
IRPA
[
Immigration and Refugee Protection
Act
] is principally a question of mixed fact and law ...". This issue is therefore reviewable on the standard of reasonableness.
