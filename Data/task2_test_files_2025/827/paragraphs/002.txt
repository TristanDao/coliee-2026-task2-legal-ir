The applicant is a young woman who is a citizen of Haiti. She made an asylum claim in the United States, which was denied in 2005 and then subsequently made a refugee claim in Canada based on her membership to a particular group, namely that of women from Haiti who have been raped and who have reported the rape to the authorities. The applicant also claimed to be a person in need of protection as she claims to face a risk to her life or cruel and unusual punishment should she be returned to Haiti.
