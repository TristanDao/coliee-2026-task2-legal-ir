Novopharm indicated LifeScan's statement that NOVO GLUCOSE strips provide "whole blood test results" was incorrect because those strips are calibrated for plasma-serum results. It said its strips were manufactured under a licence from LifeScan Inc. U.S. (not specifically mentioning DSI) and added that it did not "expect LifeScan Canada to endorse its NOVO GLUCOSE strips, as this product is in direct sales competition with ONE TOUCH® strips". It said the alternative strip had been independently tested numerous times and that the tests showed that the alternative strip performs comparably to ONE TOUCH® strips.
