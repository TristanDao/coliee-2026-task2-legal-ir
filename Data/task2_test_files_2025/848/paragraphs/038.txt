Novopharm, in its affidavit material on this application, stated that LifeScan had been in touch with at least two provincial formularies in order to prevent the listing of its NOVO GLUCOSE strips. Reference was specifically made to a March 7, 2000 letter written to the Associate Director, Drug Programs Branch, Ontario Ministry of Health and Long-term Care by LifeScan's Manager, Professional and Government Affairs in Canada. In that letter, LifeScan Canada raised quality and accuracy issues. It also raised labelling issues similar to the ones previously raised with HPB. LifeScan Canada said the strips were not comparable or substantially similar to the ONE TOUCH test strips. LifeScan appended the studies previously sent to HPB.
