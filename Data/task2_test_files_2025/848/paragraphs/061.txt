Laura Furdas is a Toronto pharmacist who, in 1992, joined the Court Pharmacy at Centenary Health Centre. This pharmacy has six pharmacists and nine technicians and is one of the few that specializes in meeting the needs of diabetes patients in Metro Toronto. She described the advice diabetes patients receive in metre selection and the training they receive on how to use the metre including how to use the control solution and how to check the test strips as well as troubleshooting. She identified several factors in selecting a metre and indicated that most patients coming to her pharmacy will have consulted with a physician or a diabetes educator and will have been advised on which metre to purchase.
