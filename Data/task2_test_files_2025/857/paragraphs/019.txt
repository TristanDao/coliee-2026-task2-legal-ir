Relying on s. 18.5 (and formerly s. 29) of the
Federal Court Act
and on s. 152(8) of the

Albion Transportation Research Corp. et al. v. Minister of National Revenue
, [1998] 1 F.C. 78; 133 F.T.R. 250 (T.D.);
Greene v. Minister of National Revenue
(1995), 189 N.R. 393; 95 D.T.C. 5684 (F.C.A.);
Optical Recording Corp. v. Canada
, supra; and
Fleming Estate v. Minister of National Revenue
, [1984] 2 F.C. 331; 61 N.R. 113 (F.C.A.)).
