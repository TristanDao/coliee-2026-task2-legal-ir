Joyal, J.
: This is an application to vacate a direction by the Minister of National Revenue made pursuant to paragraph 225.2(1) of the Income Tax Act, S.C. 1970-71-72, c. 63. It arises from the enactment by Parliament in 1985 of certain amendments to the Income Tax Act limiting the authority of the Minister of National Revenue to enforce payment of taxes owing on an assessment until there has been a final determination of the tax payable.
