Shortly after her death Mr. Williams was arrested by the Toronto Police and charged with a number of offences related to the use or possession of fraudulent credit cards. The Toronto Police alerted the immigration authorities and Mr. Williams was detained on August 16, 2006. An inadmissibility report was issued pursuant to section 44(1) of the Act. The applicant was later released on a $5000 bond posted by the sister of his deceased wife.
