The duty of fairness in the context of a deferral request is low; nonetheless, it has been held that even on a low standard, it is contrary to the duty of fairness to render a decision relying on extrinsic evidence on a material point that was not provided by the applicant:
Level (Litigation Guardian of) v. Canada (Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness)
, 2008 FC 227.
