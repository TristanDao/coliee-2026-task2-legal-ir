One might argue that the officer erred in focusing on the applicant's wife's heart issue and not on her pregnancy; indeed, the pending birth may well have been a stronger consideration. The Court of Appeal in
Baron
, at para. 51, approved the observation in
Wang
that a pending birth may be a valid reason for deferral. In this case the officer notes that the applicant's wife is expecting in three months time and that she has 5 other children for whom she and her husband care, but he fails to set out his thoughts on whether this fact alone warrants a deferral.
