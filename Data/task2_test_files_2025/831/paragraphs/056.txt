When a decision such as this has been set aside, it is usual for the Court to refer the application back to be decided by a different officer. In this instance, such an order is inappropriate because the baby that was expected has now been born, presumably Mrs. Williams' heart issues have been resolved, the business situation may have changed, and it is now almost three years since the H&C application was filed - more than twice the processing time indicated by the Minister.
