The applicant's claim for refugee status was dismissed by the Convention Refugee Determination Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board ("CRDD") on July 24, 1992. He then applied to this court for leave to commence an application for judicial review of that decision. Leave was denied on March 10, 1993. A motion for a reconsideration of that denial was filed on September 30, 1993. The application for a reconsideration was dismissed on December 20, 1993.
