The applicant's motion relates to his claim to be a Convention refugee. He comes from Egypt and states that in that country he is persecuted by Islamic fundamentalist groups, with the tacit acquiescence of the Egyptian government because of what they characterize as his heretical religious beliefs. His affidavit reads in part:
"9. During the 1970's, I wrote articles in the student newspapers criticising the behaviour of the individuals in Egyptian society. I believe in a philosophy based upon science and philosophical study, and believe the emphasis on religion as a political approach plays a destructive role in my society. During my summers, I travelled through various provinces in Egypt attending different political and religious meeting [sic]. I used logic to speak out against dogmatic political and religious activities, trying to encourage people to adopt more scientific approaches to their social values. ..."
