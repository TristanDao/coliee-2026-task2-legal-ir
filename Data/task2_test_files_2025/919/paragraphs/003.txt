The Applicants seek judicial review of the decision of the officer on the basis that it was unreasonable. It is alleged that the officer ignored material evidence in regard to the risk that would be faced by the Applicants in returning to Guyana where they would be assumed to have wealth and thus be targeted, and in the officer's assessment of their establishment in Canada. Secondly, it is alleged that the officer's assessment of the best interests of the children was unreasonable as it failed to consider the family support and social connections the older children had in Canada and the lack thereof in Guyana.
