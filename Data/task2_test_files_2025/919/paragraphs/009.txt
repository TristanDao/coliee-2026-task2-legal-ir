In my view, the officer committed an error of law in her analysis of the best interests of the Canadian born child, Sarah. The proper focus of those considerations must be on the child herself. In this case, the officer failed to focus on the child and the hardship the removal of her family, with or without her, would have on her. Instead the officer focused her analysis on the hardship to the family if they were removed from Canada, with or without the child.
