The visa officer reasonably determined, in the respondent's submission, that the certificate from the Jiansu Provincial Guidance Committee of Self-Taught Higher Education, which is a two year self-taught English language program, did not require a university entrance standard as a prerequisite to enter. This finding, in the respondent's submission, is based upon the visa officer's numerous years of processing applications from the People's Republic of China and his understanding of what type of courses require university entrance.
