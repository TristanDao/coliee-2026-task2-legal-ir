In my view, the problems identified by the applicants with respect to the contents of Apotex Inc.'s application record are factors that they must address, if they wish, in their supplementary application record to be filed under rule 1608 in accordance with the schedule established by Rouleau, J., in his order of July 9, 1996, and not in an interlocutory motion that necessarily delays the advancement of the case. As Strayer, J.A., noted in
Pharmacia
, at p. 53:
"This all reinforces the view that the focus in judicial review is on moving the application along to the hearing stage as quickly as possible. This ensures that objections to the originating notice can be dealt with promptly in the context of consideration of the merits of the case."
