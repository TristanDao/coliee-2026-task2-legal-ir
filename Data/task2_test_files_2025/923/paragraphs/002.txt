On April 4, 1996, pursuant to s. 55.2 of the
Patent Act
and s. 6 of the
Patented Medicines (Notice of Compliance) Regulations
, SOR/93-133 (the
Regulations
), the applicants filed an application for judicial review (the application) to prohibit the Minister of National Health and Welfare from issuing a notice of compliance to Apotex Inc. until after the expiration of four patents, namely 1,062,257; 1,096,863; 1,096,864 and 1,172,169. The application was filed in response to the sending by Apotex Inc. of a notice of allegation (s. 5(3)(b) of the
Regulations
) dated February 21, 1996, which stated the following:
"Patents 1096863 and 1096864 have no claim for the medicine (acyclovir) itself or the use of the medicine."
