The respondent contends that the delay is systemic and cannot be attributed to the Minister. Relying on
Singh (Surjit) v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, [1998] F.T.R. Uned. 218; 47 Imm. L.R.(2d) 83 (T.D.), counsel says that the test has not been met because the embassy made inquiries into the application, work continued on the file, and the officers involved acted in good faith. In short, it is a file in progress and therefore, the delay is justified.
