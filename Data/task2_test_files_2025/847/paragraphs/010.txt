In his affidavit, the applicant described the information he gave to the visa officer concerning the demand in Canada for Chinese speaking police officers (in both Toronto and York). He states that he told the visa officer he had received a long distance telephone call in Hong Kong, from Constable Fernando of the Metropolitan Toronto Police, in relation to his application. He states that he had been informed by his counsel, Mr. Mak, that the police normally do not make such international telephone calls unless they are extremely interested in an applicant. The visa officer denies that any statement was made to her describing a telephone call from a Constable Fernando.
