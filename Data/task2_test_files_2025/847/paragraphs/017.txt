The exercise of discretion is nonetheless exceptional in the sense described by Mr. Justice Evans in
Chen v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
(1999), 166 F.T.R. 78 (T.D.), and
Lu v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
