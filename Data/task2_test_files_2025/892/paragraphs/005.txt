The solution to the dilemma of counsel for the applicants is, in counsel's submission, the relief requested in these applications. The substance of the affidavits of Mr. Biondi that are sought to be substituted for those of Mr. Henein is substantially identical to the substance of the Henein affidavits. Counsel for the applicants argues that, as a result, the respondents will not be prejudiced by the withdrawal of the Henein affidavits and the substitution of the Biondi affidavits since cross-examination on the Biondi affidavits will be based on substantially identical substance.
