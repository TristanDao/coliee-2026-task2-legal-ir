Counsel for the respondents Apotex Inc. and NuPharm Inc. seeks to cross-examine Mr. Henein on his affidavits. Counsel for the applicants takes the position that, in order to prepare Mr. Henein for cross-examination, discussions between counsel and Mr. Henein are necessary. Counsel for the applicants is concerned that it would be unethical, in view of Mr. Henein's new position, for counsel to communicate with him since such communication would, in counsel's opinion, inevitably require discussion with respect to his responsibilities and activities since joining Novopharm Limited.
