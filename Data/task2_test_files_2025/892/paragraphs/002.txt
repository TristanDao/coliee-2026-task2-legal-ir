The two applications came on for hearing before me at Toronto on Monday, the 27th day of March, 1995. I adjourned the applications to allow the applicants to file further and better evidence in support of the applications and to allow the respondents in turn, to file evidence, reply or give notice of intent to cross-examine with respect to any further evidence. The applicants filed further evidence. The respondents notified the Registry of the court that they did not intend to file evidence, to reply or to cross-examine on the further evidence but rather wished to make further argument on the motions. Further argument was heard on Tuesday, April 18, 1995, by teleconference, with counsel and a registrar in attendance at the court in Toronto and this judge and a registrar in attendance in judge's chambers in Ottawa. At the close of the further hearing, I indicated that I would reserve and provide my decisions and brief reasons at a later date.
