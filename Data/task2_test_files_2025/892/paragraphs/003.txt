The factual situations underlying the two applications are identical. Nabil Henein, at the time he swore the affidavits now sought to be withdrawn, was employed as Director of Regulatory Affairs at Syntex Inc. He ceased to be employed by Syntex Inc. on November 29, 1994. On January 16, 1995, he commenced employment at Novopharm Limited as manager, International Regulatory Affairs. He continued to hold that position at the time these applications were brought. It was common ground between counsel before me that Novopharm Limited is a competitor of Syntex Inc., Apotex Inc. and Nu-Pharm Inc. It is a respondent in litigation before this court of a substantially similar nature to the subject matter of the two applications underlying the instant applications, with Syntex Inc., or its successor in title, being the party in adverse interest to Novopharm Limited.
