Here, the applicants allege that, because of the affiant Nabil Henein's new employment with a corporation involved in litigation with one or more of the applicants or their successors in title, it would be unethical for counsel for the applicants to communicate with Mr. Henein and it would therefore be impossible to effectively prepare him for cross-examination on his affidavits. It was suggested in argument that this would prejudice the applicants. It was also suggested that substitution of the affidavits as proposed, would in no sense prejudice the respondents.
