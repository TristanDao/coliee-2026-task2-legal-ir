I do not draw the same conclusions from a comparison of the CAIPs notes and the handwritten notes as counsel for the applicant draws. The CAIPs notes were entered a week after the interview. The visa officer would likely remember the interview fairly clearly at that time, especially given the handwritten notes she had kept. The handwritten notes would not be exhaustive of all that was said at the interview. In this case, there is no reason to doubt the credibil­ity of the CAIPs notes.
