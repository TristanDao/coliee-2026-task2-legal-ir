While the questions do not ask a pros­pective immigrant to commit to a specific business venture, they do ask for "an outline of the nature of the business you plan to establish". The applicant's "plan" was "to investigate" the market. He identified three possible areas of business activity: exporting leisure music CDs from Canada to China; importing barbecues from China into Canada; using Canadian aluminum or hot or cold rolled steel for the manufacture of barbecues in China.
