In
Gitxsan Treaty Society v. Hospital Employees' Union et al.
, [2000] 1 F.C. 135; 249 N.R. 37 (F.C.A.), the Court of Appeal held that the notice requirement is mandatory, with two possible exceptions: where the Attorneys General consent or where there has been de facto notice. On the record before me there was no consent to waive the notice requirement and no basis to find that there had been de facto notice.
