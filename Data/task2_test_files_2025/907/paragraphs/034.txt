The argument that paragraph 117(9)(d) is ultra vires was thoroughly canvassed by Justice Kelen, in
De Guzman v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
(2004), 257 F.T.R. 290 (F.C.). I do not propose to repeat the analysis that he conducted in arriving at the conclusion that the regulation was not ultra vires. I adopt his reasoning for the purposes of this application.
