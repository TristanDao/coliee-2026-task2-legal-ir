Mr. Azizi entered Canada as a Convention refugee seeking resettlement on August 21, 2001. He was sponsored by the World University Services Canada ("WUSC") for study at a Canadian post-secondary institution. His record of landing - with a signed certification that the statements it contains are true and correct - makes no mention of his wife or daughters. In answer to the question "Have you any dependants other than those listed above?", he answered "No". Earlier, in his application for permanent residence in Canada submitted on February 9, 2001, the box indicating "never married" was marked and "N/A" was noted next to questions relating to the date and place of marriage and personal details of dependants.
