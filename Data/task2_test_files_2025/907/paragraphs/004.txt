Mr. Azizi applied to sponsor his wife and daughters in April 2003. The application was initially denied on May 30, 2003 but forwarded to the post in Islamabad for further consideration. In a letter to Mr. Azizi and a separate letter to Wahida dated November 11, 2003, a visa officer in Islamabad determined that Wahida was not eligible for sponsorship. She was not a member of the family class because of the operation of paragraph 117(9)(d). At the time her sponsor applied for permanent residence, she was a "non-accompanying family member" and "was not examined." Although not addressed specifically in the letter, the decision applied equally to the two daughters.
