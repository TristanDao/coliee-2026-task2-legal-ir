In
Becker v. Minister of National Revenue
(2004), 323 N.R. 195; 2004 FCA 186, the Court of Appeal stated that the notice requirement is not a mere formality that can be ignored or waived by the Court. It is a matter that goes to the jurisdiction of the Court to hear an issue. Nor is it open to the Court to grant an adjournment to enable service of a notice. See also
Giagnocavo v. Minister of National Revenue
(1995), 189 N.R. 225 (F.C.A.).
