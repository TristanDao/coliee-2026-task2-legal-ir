Leaving aside any distinction that might exist between couples who are separated by a two hour drive and those who are separated by an ocean, the most significant issue is that the officer's notes do not reference the explanations provided by the applicant and her husband. As stated earlier, the notes can be relied upon only as reasons for the decision. They cannot be used as evidence of what was said at the interview. Thus, the evidence of the applicant and her husband stands uncontradicted in this respect and I can only conclude that the officer made his decision without regard to this evidence. The explanations provided as to why the couple were not living together at the time of the interview, which were relevant, appear not to have been considered.
