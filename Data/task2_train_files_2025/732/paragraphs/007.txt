Mr. Leach grieved the involuntary transfer recommendation. On January 3, 2002, after considering Mr. Leach's grievance, the Warden of Fenbrook approved the involuntary transfer from Fenbrook to Joyceville. The transfer was approved on the grounds that Mr. Leach did not meet the security level at Fenbrook and that Fenbrook did not offer sex offender treatment. Mr. Leach was notified that the transfer decision could be appealed to the second level of the inmate grievance procedure but no appeal was taken.
