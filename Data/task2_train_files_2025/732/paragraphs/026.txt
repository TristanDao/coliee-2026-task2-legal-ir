In my view, the grievance process set out in the
CCRA
and the
Regulations
does afford an adequate alternative remedy. This is so because it is possible for Mr. Leach to address the substance of the alleged breaches of the
Charter
within that process. Potential success is available without resort to subsection 24(1) of the
Charter
. In other words, success is not solely dependent upon relief under subsection 24(1) and, judicial review from a final decision in the grievance process remains available.
