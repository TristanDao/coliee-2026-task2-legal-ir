Use in Canada is indicated for the wares marked (6) to be at least since January 1973. Once again I am unable to determine from Schedule "D" the date of first use in Canada of "printer ribbons". The information contained in Schedules "A" through "D" set out the date of first use of IBM trademarks and designs as they relate to a number of IBM's wares and services, but not to the printer ribbons specifically noted in para. 11.
