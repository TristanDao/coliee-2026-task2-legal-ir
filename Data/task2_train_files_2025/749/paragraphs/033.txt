I am of the view that Jackett, C.J.'s, comments are equally applicable to the case before me. This is not a situation where a single instance of infringement is alleged, but "continuous" infringement. The plaintiffs in para. 20 have alleged a general carrying on of business by the defendants. In the words of Jackett, C.J., the defendants must know what business they have carried on and they do not need any particulars with respect to the general allegation.
