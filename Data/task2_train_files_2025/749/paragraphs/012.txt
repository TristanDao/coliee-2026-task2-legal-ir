At issue in this action are the IBM trademarks associated with printer ribbons known as "IBM
®
'930 Ribbons". Schedule "A" is a copy of trademark Registration No. UCA38407 and provides various dates of first use for wares and services, including accounting and statistical machines; calculating machines; clocks; typewriters, parts and accessories for typewriters and "office supplies used in relation to the above described wares, and including inter alia inks, carbon papers, inking ribbons, carbon ribbons, cards and printed forms." There is no specific mention in Schedule "A" of the "printer ribbons". Although, "printer ribbons" may be included in "inking ribbons" or "carbon ribbons" (and I make this statement with proviso that I am not making a decision on this point) it is not clear from the registration what the date of first use is for any specific ware.
