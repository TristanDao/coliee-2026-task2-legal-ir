In response to the defendants' request for particulars, the plaintiffs note paras. 20 to 31 specify what wares are at issue. In terms of which of the defendants the passing off allegations relate to, the beginning of para. 32 makes specific reference to the defendants Printech and Stalwart and para. 34 states in part "the defendants Stalwart and Printech, together, have committed the acts complained of ...". Further, para. 36 states "As a result, the defendants Riyaz Jamal and Karim Jamal personally, have committed the acts complained of ...", namely passing off. For the same reasons given with respect to para. 32(c), I am satisfied that paras. 20 to 36 provided sufficient details to allow the defendants to plead to the Statement of Claim.
