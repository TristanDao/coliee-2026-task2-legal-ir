The defendants seek particulars with respect to allegations contained in paras. 11, 12, 13, 15, 32(b), 32(c), 32(d), 40, 17, 18, 19 and 38 of the Statement of Claim. The defendants submit they are entitled to obtain particulars concerning vague, ambiguous and imprecise allegations contained in the Statement of Claim. In support of their application the defendants filed the affidavit of Karim Jamal (the Jamal affidavit), president and one of the directors of Printech Ribbons Inc. (Printech). Exhibit "A" to the Jamal affidavit is a letter dated February 24, 1994, from the defendants' counsel addressed to the plaintiffs' counsel requesting certain particulars. The particulars sought by the motion are the same as those set out in the demand letter. Exhibit "B" to the Jamal affidavit is a letter, dated March 23, 1994, from the plaintiffs' counsel to the defendants' counsel giving or refusing to give the particulars requested. In essence, the letter sets out the plaintiffs' position with respect to the particulars requested.
