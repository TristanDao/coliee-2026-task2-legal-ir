Once again, the defendants have relied upon a case dealing with an application to strike a Statement of Claim in a passing off action, on the basis that it discloses no reasonable cause of action. I have no dispute with the comments quoted in the context of an application to strike a Statement of Claim as not disclosing a reasonable cause of action, unfortunately, the application before me involves a demand for particulars for the purposes of pleading. As such, the case is not applicable to the motion before me.
