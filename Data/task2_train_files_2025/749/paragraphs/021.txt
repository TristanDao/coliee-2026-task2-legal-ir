The defendants also seek particulars as to how each plaintiff made each trademark relating to printer ribbons, well known in Canada. In response, the plaintiffs refer to paras. 9 through 14 of the Statement of Claim, which make reference to licensed use by IBM Canada and Lexmark, as well as advertisement, display and promotion of the trademarks throughout Canada in association with IBM wares, and in particular printer ribbons. Further, the plaintiffs allege such use is in association with a public notice of licensed use and presumed to be under the control of IBM Corp. as to the character or quality of the IBM wares.
