Therefore, the defendants' motion for particulars is granted on the following limited terms:
1) the plaintiffs shall furnish particulars, in writing, with respect to para. 11 of the Statement of Claim, indicating the date of first use of "printer ribbons" in Canada.
2) the plaintiffs shall furnish particulars, in writing, with respect to para. 15 of the Statement of Claim, indicating since when each of the trademarks associated with printer ribbons have been used by each plaintiff and indicating whether IBM marks have been well known in the whole of Canada or in a specific area or areas of Canada in association with IBM wares and in particular printer ribbons.
