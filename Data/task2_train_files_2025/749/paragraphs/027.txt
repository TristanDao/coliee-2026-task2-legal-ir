In response to the defendants' request for particulars, the plaintiffs note to the wording of para. 32: "By the actions and conduct as aforesaid". This by implication refers to the depreciation of goodwill pleaded in the Statement of Claim. The depreciation occurred as a consequence of the defendants' action and conduct set out in paras. 20 through 30. The answer to the question "when" is: after and a result of the actions and conduct of the defendants set out in the preceding paragraphs. Paragraphs 21 through 27 refer to specific dates when certain activities took place. Similarly, the answer to the question "how" is: by the actions and conduct of the defendants set out in paras. 20 through 31. Therefore, the plaintiffs have made a general allegation of offering for sale and selling or carrying on a business using the plaintiffs' trademarks by the defendants, followed by specific allegations of two transactions.
