The plaintiffs are of the view that the defendants do not require the date when each plaintiff used each IBM trademark associated with printer ribbon to plead to the Statement of Claim. The defendants' position is the same with respect to how each plaintiff has promoted the trademarks. Further, the plaintiffs maintain Schedules "A" through "D", attached to the Statement of Claim, contain copies of the registrations of the IBM trademarks at issue and each registration includes dates of first use of the trademark in association with the wares and services listed in the registration. Therefore, claim the plaintiffs, the schedules provide the date when IBM Corporation began using in Canada, each of the trademarks identified in the Statement of Claim.
