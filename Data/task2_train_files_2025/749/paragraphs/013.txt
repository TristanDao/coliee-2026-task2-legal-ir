Schedule "B" is a copy of trademark Registration No. 169,850 and refers to a number of wares and services. The wares listed include: information handling machines and systems, accounting and statistical machines, calculating machines, computers, printing machines; computer programs; typewriters and parts and attachments for typewriters; printers composing equipment; dictating equipment and "(6) Supplies and accessories used in relation to the above described wares, namely inks; carbon papers; inking ribbons; spools and containers for inking ribbons; carbon ribbons cards ...". Once again I can find no specific reference to "printer ribbons". The date of first use for inking and carbon ribbons is given as February 14, 1930. The question becomes is this also the date of first use for the "printer ribbons" referred to in the Statement of Claim?
