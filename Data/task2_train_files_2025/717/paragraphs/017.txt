The letter dated December 29, 2003, which invited the applicant to the assessment interview on March 9, 2006, stated clearly the purpose of the interview. The applicant was informed in significant detail that the assessment interview was final and that a decision would be made on the applicant’s case at the interview, based on the documents presented at that time. While the Visa Officer did exercise her discretion and went beyond her duty of fairness to ask the applicant if he had further proof of the source of his income, opportunity which he declined, it would be to put too onerous a task on the Visa Officer to go beyond the stated purpose in the letter of invitation to the assessment interview.
