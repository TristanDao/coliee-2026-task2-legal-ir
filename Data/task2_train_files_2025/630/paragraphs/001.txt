Blanchard, J.
: This is an application for judicial review of the decision of a labour adjudicator appointed pursuant to Division XIV, Part III of the
Canada Labour Code
, R.S.C. 1985, c. L-2, as amended (the "
Code
"). In his decision rendered March 1, 2002, the adjudicator determined that the dismissal of the respondent was unjust and ordered that she be compensated with an award of seven months' wages. The applicant maintains that the adjudicator did not have jurisdiction to hear the claim and seeks to have the decision quashed.
