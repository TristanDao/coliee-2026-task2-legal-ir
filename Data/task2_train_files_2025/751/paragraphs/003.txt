In 1994, Apotex filed a NDS for its Apo-Omeprazole 20 capsules. In 1997, Apotex re-filed its NDS as an abbreviated new drug submission (ANDS). Pursuant to subsection C.08.002.1 of the
Food and Drug Regulations,
C.R.C., c-870, an ANDS may be filed instead of an NDS where the new drug is the pharmaceutical equivalent of a Canadian reference product.
