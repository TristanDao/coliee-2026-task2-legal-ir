At page 209, Cattanach, J., supports the proposition that s. 27 and s. 36 costs are actually two sets of costs. Although they may be combined in one bill of costs, it may be required that they be segregated so that different scales may be applied to them. In my view, however, these different scales would only apply if the award of costs by the court under s. 36 were party and party costs or otherwise contained one or several directions.
