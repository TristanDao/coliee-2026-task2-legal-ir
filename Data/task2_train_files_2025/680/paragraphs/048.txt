I must now return to the specific matters raised in the appeal respecting the Prothonotary's order of taxation. The Prothonotary ruled that the interest charged by Strung Real Estate Limited, the appraisers on behalf of the plaintiffs, should be taxed for the period from the date of the account to the date of the commencement of the action. Although the agreed interest charged was at the rate of 18% per annum, the Prothonotary allowed interest pursuant to s. 33, i.e. 13%. With respect to a further account from Strung Real Estate Limited, accrued interest charges of some $600 were disallowed.
