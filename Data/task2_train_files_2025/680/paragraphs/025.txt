Incidentally, I should note in that respect that Rouleau, J., does not adopt the wording found in s. 36(2) which speaks of "costs of and incident to the proceeding" but of legal, appraisal and other costs reasonably incurred in asserting a claim for compensation, the language found in s. 27. In either event, I should find that the distinction in the wording is without a difference. In either case, a taxing officer must subject all items of a bill to the test of reasonableness as well as to the test of relevancy.
