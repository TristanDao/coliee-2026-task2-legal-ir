The taxation of costs dealt with an expropriation matter where certain provisions of the Expropriation Act, R.S.C. 1970 (1st Supp.), c. 16, provide for costs to be awarded to any person whose property has been forcibly taken. It also deals with an order of Rouleau, J., dated November 19, 1985, wherein the plaintiffs were entitled to recover from the Crown legal, appraisal and other costs reasonably incurred in assisting their claims for compensation as determined by taxation on a solicitor and client basis."
