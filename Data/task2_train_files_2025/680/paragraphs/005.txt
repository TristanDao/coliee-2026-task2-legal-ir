Without going into the details at this time, the plaintiffs were required to claim a number of separate items on their bill of costs many of them dealing with services performed by their appraisers and accountants and extending over a remarkably long period. Some of these accounts had been left outstanding for some time and interest charges had accrued on them. Some of these accounts were in respect of services rendered prior to the institution of the action and some afterwards. In brief, some of the costs were apparently the kind of costs provided under s. 27 of the Expropriation Act and the others were costs provided under s. 36, i.e. costs incidental to the proceedings in this court.
