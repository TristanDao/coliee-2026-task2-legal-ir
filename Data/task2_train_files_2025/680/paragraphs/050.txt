The breakdown of these accounting costs, in view of my findings might of course no longer be required except in the event that a taxing officer might decide that a different rate of interest should apply for costs prior or subsequent to the institution of proceedings. The tests imposed under either s. 27 or s. 36 of the Act would be the same. Both seek to indemnify the owner, subject only to control by a taxing officer so that the reasonableness of the charges for purposes of asserting or claiming for compensation (s. 27) or for purposes of and incident to the proceedings (s. 36) may be ascertained.
