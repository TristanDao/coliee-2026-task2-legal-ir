I do not believe that such an issue is an issue of law. I should think that it is a matter for the taxing officer's discretion. I should venture to suggest that allowing or refusing interest charges accruing on certain items until the whole bill of costs is finally settled is one among any number of elements which a taxing officer must consider. In the matter before me, whether he must fix "costs reasonably incurred" under s. 27 or "costs reasonably incurred" pursuant to Rouleau, J.'s, order of November 19, 1985, the fields of enquiry he must pursue would appear to me to be the same and should not be subjected to more didactic rules than are reasonably required so as to give the parties some guidance as to what they might expect.
