The other item on the plaintiffs' bill of costs covered accounting fees submitted by Turner, McCabe & Associates and by C.W. Williams, C.A. One statement of account indicated an interest charge at 24% per annum on an account of $10,020 and the other statement indicated interest at 24% on an account of $4,845. Both accounts together with interest amounted to $20,380.16. The learned Prothonotary would appear to have assessed that item at $6,675. The record, however, does not disclose the basis on which this amount was fixed nor is there evidence as to how much of the accounting services were performed prior or subsequent to the commencement of the proceedings.
