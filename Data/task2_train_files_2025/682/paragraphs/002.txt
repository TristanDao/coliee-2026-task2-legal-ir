The regulatory scheme established under the amendments to the Patent Act, R.S.C. 1985, c. P-4, as amended S.C. 1993, c. 2, and by the
Patented Medicines (Notice of Compliance) Regulations
has been described in several decisions. See, for example,
Bayer AG and Miles Canada Inc. v. Canada (Minister of Health and Welfare) and Apotex Inc.
(1993), 163 N.R. 183; 51 C.P.R.(3d) 329 (F.C.A.), and
Eli Lilly & Co. v. Novopharm Ltd. et al.
(1995), 95 F.T.R. 96; 60 C.P.R.(3d) 417 (T.D.). I refer to those cases for the descriptions found therein and will not repeat the description here.
