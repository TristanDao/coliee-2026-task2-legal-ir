All of the applicant's jewellery was seized for appraisal with the exception of the gold bracelet with white stones which was seized for failure to report (the "bracelet"). The applicant informed the BSO that the bracelet was purchased in Afghanistan for the equivalent of approximately $423.88 (Canadian dollars). The BSO accepted the applicant's valuation of this bracelet and assessed a penalty of $127.16, constituting 30% of the bracelet's value. The applicant paid this sum and Customs returned the bracelet to her.
