With regards to the applicant's argument that some of the jewellery was actually taken with her from Canada to Afghanistan, the Minister considered this argument, but found that she was not able to provide evidence of lawful importation or Canadian origin of the jewellery. This determination was made based on the Minister's examination of the submitted receipts. The receipts were found not to be specific enough to draw any solid conclusions in favour of the applicant (Minister's decision, page 2).
