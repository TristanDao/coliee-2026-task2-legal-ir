Finally, the Court determines that there was no breach of procedural fairness in this case. The applicant was notified in due course that she was in contravention of the Act, was made aware of why and how she could contradict or contest the Minister's findings. She was given ample time to provide reliable documents or explanations to assert that the Minister's decision was unreasonable. She was also informed at all times how the decision was rendered.
