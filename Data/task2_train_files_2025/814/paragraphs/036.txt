Finally, the Applicant says his apprehension of bias is heightened by the ID’s finding at para 25 of her decision that, “a baseless allegation of bias is unbecoming conduct for a lawyer.”  Counsel submits that this is a “personal attack on counsel for advocating fearlessly and without frivolity for his client is indeed further evidence of the ID’s bias.” With respect, I disagree. Unjustified attacks on a decision-maker such as the ID on the basis of actual bias cannot be encouraged. I am not persuaded it is an error to state that a baseless allegation of bias is unbecoming conduct.
