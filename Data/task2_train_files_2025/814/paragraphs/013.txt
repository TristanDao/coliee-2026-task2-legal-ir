Findings of the terrorist nature of an organization or an individual’s membership in a particular organization are reviewable on the reasonableness standard: Kanagendren v Canada (Citizenship and Immigration), 2015 FCA 86 at para 11 per Dawson JA; Suresh v Canada (Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness), 2017 FC 28 at para 44 per Mosley J; Mirmahaleh v Canada (Citizenship and Immigration), 2015 FC 1085 at para 15 per Gascon J; and see my decision in Ali v Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration), 2017 FC 182 [Ali] at para 22.
