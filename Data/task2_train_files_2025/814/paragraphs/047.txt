The ID considered all this and concluded that while the Jubo Dal enjoyed some autonomy, its existence and legitimacy is based on the constitution of the BNP. The ID further concluded that the “[t]he raison d’être of the Jubo Dal is to further the BNP. It falls under the discipline of the BNP and even shares in BNP governance in that the Jubo Dal secretary sits on the BNP’s national executive committee.”
