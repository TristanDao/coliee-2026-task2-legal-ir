The Applicant submits that the Officer's finding that she did not have at least one year of work experience as a Cook was a reviewable error because:
1) The Applicant provided uncontradicted credible evidence that she had been employed as a Cook for more than one year, during which time she had performed a substantial number of the main duties of the occupation of Cook as found in the relevant NOC;
2) The Applicant provided uncontradicted credible evidence of the kitchen layout at the establishment of her current employer and the Officer does not question the credibility of that evidence;
3) The Applicant's current employer confirmed by telephone that the Applicant was employed as a Cook. The Officer erred in law by not taking this evidence into account as the purpose of the call was to corroborate the Applicant's employment experience.
