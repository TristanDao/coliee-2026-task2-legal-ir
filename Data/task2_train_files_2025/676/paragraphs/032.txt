But the Officer leaves out of account the crucial fact that Mr. Singh confirmed most of what the Applicant had said. He provided reliable evidence (not questioned by the Officer) that the Applicant worked for him in the role of cook. The Officer remains silent on evidence that points away from the conclusion she came to regarding the Applicant's credibility. She took that part of Mr. Singh's evidence which she felt supported her conclusions but ignored the confirmatory evidence he gave regarding the Applicant's experience as a cook. The Officer does not explain in her Decision why she ignored evidence that was directly material to the decision she had to make and which undermined her own conclusions on credibility.
