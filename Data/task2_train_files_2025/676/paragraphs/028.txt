It is well established that credibility issues and the weight to be given to evidence are matters for the officer concerned and must be given significant deference by a reviewing court:
Sarkissian v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, [2003] F.C.J. No. 489 (F.C.) at para. 9;
Oladipo v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
2008 FC 366.
