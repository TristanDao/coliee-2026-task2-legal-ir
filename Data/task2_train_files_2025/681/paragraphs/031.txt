Rule 81(1) of the
Federal Court Rules, 1998
provides that affidavits shall be confined to facts within the personal knowledge of the deponent, except when filed on a motion. It goes without saying that the affidavit here in question was not filed on a motion. To the extent that it is made on belief, Rule 81(2) provides that an adverse inference may be drawn from the failure of a party, here the applicant, to provide evidence of persons having personal knowledge of material facts. I draw such an adverse inference.
