Counsel referred to Fricke and Seaton Timber Ltd. v. Michell et al., [1986] 1 W.W.R. 544 (B.C.S.C.), to Mitchell and Milton Management Ltd. v. Sandy Bay Indian Band et al., [1983] 5 W.W.R. 117; 22 Man.R.(2d) 286 (Q.B.), upheld on appeal [1986] 2 W.W.R. 477; 39 Man.R.(2d) 180 (C.A.), and to Fayerman Brothers Limited v. Peter Ballantyne Band and Peter Ballantyne Band Council (1984), 36 Sask.R. 76 (Q.B.). In these cases funds paid to an Indian Band by Her Majesty, either through the federal or provincial government as the case was, were not subject to a garnishing order by virtue of s. 90(1) of the Indian Act.
