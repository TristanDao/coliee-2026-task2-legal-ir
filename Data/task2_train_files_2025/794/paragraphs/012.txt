In the circumstances, I grant the application by the judgment debtor, the employer, Westbank Indian Band, that the garnishing order made April 23, 1990, directed to the garnishee the Bank of Montreal be set aside and that any and all moneys paid into court pursuant to the said garnishing order including accrued interest be returned to the judgment debtor, the Westbank Indian Band. The judgment debtor shall have costs of its application. At the same time an order goes dismissing the application by the complainants that the garnishee appear and pay to the complainants the debt due from the garnishee to the judgment debtor. Costs in the case of both motions are awarded to the judgment debtor.
Application allowed.
