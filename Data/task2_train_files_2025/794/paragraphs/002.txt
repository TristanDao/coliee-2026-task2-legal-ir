Since the order of McNair, J., the garnishee had paid funds in the amount stipulated into court pursuant to rule 2300(4). In these circumstances the garnishee did not appear but an affidavit of Allan Birchard, account manager of the Bank of Montreal, Westbank branch, testified that the amount paid into court under the garnishing order was paid from a general current account of the Westbank Indian Band at that branch of the Bank of Montreal. His affidavit also appended a copy of the bank's statement of account indicating a debit of the amount paid into court on the 27th of April.
