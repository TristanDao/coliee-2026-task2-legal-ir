Ms. Thompson for the judgment creditors cited Mintuck v. Valley River Band No. 63A et al. (1977), 83 D.L.R.(3d) 324 (Man. Q.B.), where a garnishing order against funds paid to a band was upheld, but in that case the order was made in favour of one member of the Indian Band who obtained judgment against the Band. That circumstance is clearly an exception under s. 89(1) of the Indian Act to the general bar against levy or seizure of property of an Indian or a band situated on a reserve. It has no relevance to the circumstances of this case where the judgment creditors are not Indian people.
