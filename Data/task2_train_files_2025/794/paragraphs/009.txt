I have no reservations that the funds here paid into court by the garnishee were paid from moneys paid to the Indian band by the Government of Canada under an agreement between the band and Her Majesty. Under s. 90(1) of the Indian Act those funds are deemed always to be situate on a reserve, and by virtue of s. 89(1) personal property  (including funds) of a band situate on a reserve is not subject to levy, execution or garnishment in favour of any person other than an Indian or a band.
