In this case, the uncontroverted evidence before this Court is that Mr. Pramauntanyath did precisely what he was asked to do by CIC. Whether as a result of a problem with the mail system, or an error within the CIC's own offices, through no fault on the part of Mr. Pramauntanyath, the supplementary information was not put before the immigration officer at the time that his H & C application was considered. The result was that Mr. Pramauntanyath's application was not considered on the basis of a complete record. As was previously noted, the additional information may well have affected the result.
