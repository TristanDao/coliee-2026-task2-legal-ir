Mr. Pramauntanyath came to Canada from Thailand in February of 1994 on a student visa. After the school that he was attending closed, Mr. Pramauntanyath began working without authorization at a restaurant in Toronto. In April of 2000, Mr. Pramauntanyath and two partners opened their own restaurant. Mr. Pramauntanyath is a one-third owner in the operation, and works as the chef.
