As we will see later, the evidence shows that Election Supervisors and members of the Council of Elders are likely to be biased. That being said, although provision is made in the
Code
or in the accompanying Regulations for the election of a Council of Elders and Election Supervisors, those requirements were never followed in past elections; the exclusion of non-residents, although mentioned in the
Code
, was never enforced in past elections. At all times since 1991, non-residents aged 18 years and over were allowed to vote and the elections were carried out by a third independent agency.
