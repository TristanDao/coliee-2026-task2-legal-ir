The referendum was held on May 31, 1991. It was conducted by the Canadian Election Consultants, a neutral organism hired by INAC. A majority of the Band members favoured the election process. Although the particular results of the plebiscite are not part of the court's record, all parties agree that there was a broad general consensus to replace the traditional mode of selecting and appointing the members of the Council by an elective system.
