Canatonquin

Bigstone et al. v. Big Eagle et al.

Canada (Attorney General) v. Lavell
, [1974] S.C.R. 1349, at 1379, in which three other judges concurred. In that case, Laskin, J., stated that "[a] Band Council has some resemblance to the board of directors of a corporation, and if the words of s. 2(g) [of the

Bank Act

Canada Corporations Act
