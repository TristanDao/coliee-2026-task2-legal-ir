The election of an interim Council was completed on June 27, 1991. It was conducted by the same neutral agency. The defined voter eligibility in this election consisted "of all members of the Kanesatake Mohawk Band (
regardless of residence
) aged 18 years or over on voting day" (Exhibit "D" to the affidavit of Nathalie Nepton, dated December 10, 2002). Seven chiefs and one grand chief were elected for a term of ten months. One of their priorities was to develop the
Code
which could be used for the next coming election which was planned to take place at the end of April 1992. It was also expected that before the election, the Community would have adopted and ratified the proposed code.
