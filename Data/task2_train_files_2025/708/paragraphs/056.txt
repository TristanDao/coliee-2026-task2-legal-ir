Immediately following the opening statements made by Grand Chief Peltier, one of the participants, Mr. Morelely Oke, an elder, intervened and moved that "we adopt the electoral code as is now on a [sic] interim basis"; the latter having stated earlier that the draft
Code
"is a working document and that's what it's meant to do ... and I think that most of us here will realize that any trouble that we have with it will come out after and the new council can deal with the things that didn't work". The motion was seconded by Mr. Richard Gabriel who added that "the elections should continue and the new council work on the new codes [sic]" (transcript of the Minutes of the Meeting held on May 20, 1992, supra, at 3).
