In
Corbiere et al. v. Canada (Minister of Indian and Northern Affairs) et al.
, [1999] 2 S.C.R. 203; 239 N.R. 1, the court invalidated, as inconsistent with s. 15 of the
Charter
, s. 77(1) of the
Act
, which excluded off-reserve members of an Indian band from the right to vote in band council elections. The remaining question is whether the court's finding also applies to custom band elections.
