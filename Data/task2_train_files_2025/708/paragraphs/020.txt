The
Act
recognizes two methods for choosing the grand chief and councillors of a band: the first is a procedure set out in the
Act
itself; the other is by "custom", and recognizes selection processes developed by First Nations. In the latter case, First Nations determine who may vote, how the voting is carried out, and when elections are held. In the case at bar, what is really at issue is how the upcoming Band custom by-election is to be conducted and who will be entitled to vote.
