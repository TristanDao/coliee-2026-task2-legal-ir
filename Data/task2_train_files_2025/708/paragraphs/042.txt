Traditionally, prior to 1991, the selection and appointment of the grand chief and other chiefs of the Council were made by the Six Nations Traditional Hereditary Clan Mothers in accordance with the custom of the band. The Council at that time consisted of one grand chief and eight chiefs. The grand chief and two chiefs were selected from the Turtle Clan. Three chiefs were selected from the Bear Clan and three chiefs were selected from the Wolf Clan.
