As stated, this public notice of the adoption and use of the AATO Marks was done pursuant to section 9(1)(n)(iii) of the
Trade-marks Act
, which states:
"No person shall adopt in connection with a business, as a trade-mark or otherwise, any mark consisting of, or so nearly resembling as to be likely to be mistaken for, any badge, crest, emblem or mark adopted and used by any public authority, in Canada as an official mark for wares or services, in respect of which the Registrar has, at the request of His Majesty or of the university or public authority, as the case may be, given public notice of its adoption and use ..."
