In
Pushpanathan
, supra, at paragraph 36, Bastarache, J., explains that courts must show greater restraint when the
Act
requires "the consideration of numerous interests simultaneously, and the promulgation of solutions which concurrently balance benefits and costs for many different parties" than when provisions that establish rights between the parties are involved. In other words, the Court must show greater restraint in respect of a polycentric decision.
