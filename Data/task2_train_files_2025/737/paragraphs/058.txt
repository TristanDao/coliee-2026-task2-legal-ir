"While the conceptual difference between review on a correctness standard and review on a patent unreasonableness standard may be intuitive and relatively easy to observe (though in practice elements of correctness at times encroach uncomfortably into patent unreasonableness review),
the boundaries between patent unreasonableness and reasonableness simpliciter are far less clear, even at the theoretical level
." (my emphasis)
