Pinard, J.
: The applicant, a citizen of Iran, applied to the Canadian Embassy in Bonn, Germany, for permanent residency on April 5, 1993, on the ground that she was a Convention Refugee seeking resettlement. On May 19, 1993, Mr. Robert Bradley, a Canadian immigration visa officer at the Canadian Embassy in Bonn, held an interview with the applicant regarding her request. In a letter dated May 28, 1993, the visa officer determined that the applicant did not meet the requirements for immigration to Canada. The applicant seeks judicial review of that decision on the ground that the applicant was not treated fairly.
