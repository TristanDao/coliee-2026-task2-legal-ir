The applicant submits that his complaint was timely; he sent the complaint on January 22, 2010 and the fact that the Commission received his complaint 19 days later is beyond his control. The applicant also submits that the Commission unreasonably refused to excuse the short delay. Moreover, he submits that the relevant date is not January 22, 2010 because the last discriminatory act was November 23, 2009 when the respondent refused to reinstate him based on his medical report.
