The applicant's explanation to the Court - that to seek to provide this information would have only invited cross-examination and further complicated the record - is unsatisfactory. I do not accept the applicant's position that the Court should find procedural unfairness because the Commission adopted the conclusions of the Report without examining the medical evidence or the arbitration awards. The Commission considered the Report and adopted it as its reasons. The Report addressed the complaint and canvassed all the relevant factors regarding timeliness and vexatiousness; it also considered the parties' position statements, the complaint, and other information submitted by the applicant.
