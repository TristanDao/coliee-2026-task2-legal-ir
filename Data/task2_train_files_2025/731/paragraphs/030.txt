The Report concluded that the complaint was untimely pursuant to paragraph 41(1)(e). The Report found that the last alleged discriminatory act was the termination of the applicant's employment on January 22, 2009. The complaint was therefore filed beyond the one year statutory time limit. The Report also noted that: the applicant had opportunities to raise human rights concerns but did not do so until now; the applicant was represented by his union at the arbitration proceedings and also by counsel since April 2009; and he provided no reasonable explanation for the delay.
