It is alleged by Beloit that Valmet sought to circumvent the Court of Appeal decision by using a company it had incorporated in Canada on March 23, 1984, Valmet-Dominion Inc., of which Valmet was the owner of 65% of the total issued shares. Beloit was and is of the belief that Valmet had Valmet-Dominion continue to bid and receive contracts for the manufacture, sale and delivery in Canada of Sym-Press 11 press sections and by permitting, inducing, procuring and helping Valmet-Dominion, Valmet's subsidiary corporation to actually manufacture and sell same.
