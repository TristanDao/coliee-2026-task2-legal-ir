The solicitor for Valmet-Dominion admits that Valmet-Dominion is owned by Valmet and Canadian General Electric. He goes on to state:
"Valmet Oy owns a majority of the shares and fundamental decisions relating to the business of the company require unanimous approval of both shareholders. Valmet-Dominion Inc. was not a party to and did not participate in the action by your client and Valmet Oy.
"In view of the foregoing, I disagree with your statement that Valmet-Dominion Inc. will be in contempt of Court of the judgment of the Federal Court of Appeal if it proceeds as indicated."
