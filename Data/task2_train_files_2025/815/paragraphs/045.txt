Beloit alleges, and they must prove beyond a reasonable doubt, that the defendant Valmet Oy contravened the injunction of February 10, 1986. The judgment, in relation to the injunction, states:
"... the respondent is enjoined from manufacturing, using or selling or inducing others to use press sections which are within claim 1 or dependant claims 2, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 and 11 of the said patent; any articles in respondent's possession or power used, made or being made in infringement of the said claims shall be forthwith delivered up to the appellants or destroyed."
