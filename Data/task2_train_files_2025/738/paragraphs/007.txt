The respondent also points to the evidence given by the applicant that his two sons were kidnapped and tortured as a result of his political activity. The CRDD chose to disregard this evidence because it was not referred to in the Personal Information Form submitted by the applicant for his first refugee hearing. Confronted with this fact, the applicant indicated that he did not know then that they could refer to incidents involving his children. The CRDD found that the incident involving the children was simply an attempt by the applicant to embellish his claim.
