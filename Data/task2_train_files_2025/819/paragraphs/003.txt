Mr. Wang has been enrolled as a student at a variety of institutions in British Columbia since his arrival in Canada. From January 2002 through August 2004, he studied English as a Second Language (ESL) at Dorset College, Alice College, and Century College. From September 2004 through April 2005, he followed a course in aircraft maintenance at Dorset College, upon completion of which he transferred to the British Columbia Institute of Technology, where he obtained a diploma in aircraft maintenance engineering in October 2006. He then returned to Dorset College in January of 2007, enrolling in the University Transfer Program with the intention of later transferring to Acadia University to pursue a bachelor's degree in business. He did not attend the Fall 2007 semester for medical reasons, but re-enrolled in January of 2008. He withdrew from the college during the Fall 2008 semester, following the respondent's decision not to renew his study permit.
