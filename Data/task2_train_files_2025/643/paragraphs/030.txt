Issue 3
Did the visa officer make an unreasonable finding of fact with respect to the applicant's ability to perform the work?
Subsection 200(3) of the
Immigration and Refugee Protection Regulations
obliges an officer to assess the applicant and prevents an officer from issuing a work permit if there are reasonable grounds to believe that the foreign national is unable to perform the work sought. An applicant has the onus to establish that there are no reasonable grounds to believe that he or she will be unable to perform the work sought.
