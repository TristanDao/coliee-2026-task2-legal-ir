Appointments within the Public Service of Canada are governed by the provisions of the Public Service Employment Act [see footnote 2]. Section 6 thereof empowers the Public Service Commission to delegate any of its functions to deputy heads who may subdelegate to other officials. Section 10 prescribes that appointments to and within the Public Service must be based on selection according to merit as determined by the Commission. Sections 13 to 20 of the Public Service Employment Regulations empower the Commission to establish competitions for the purpose of selecting qualified candidates for a position. The names of the highest ranking candidates, as determined in the competition, are placed in order of merit on a list known as the "eligible list" which list remains valid for a determined period.
