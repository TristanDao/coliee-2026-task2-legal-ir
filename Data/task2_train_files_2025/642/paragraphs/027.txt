Under the BIA the Superintendent is responsible for supervision of the administration of all estates, with broad power to oversee the regime for management of bankruptcy and insolvency situations for the benefit of debtors, creditors and others interested in the estates. A primary means of supervision is through trustee licensing and regulation of the work of trustees. Under section 5 of the Act the Superintendent's supervisory responsibilities include:
