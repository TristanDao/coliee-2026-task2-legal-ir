As earlier noted, the time this application was heard, in September 2004, and in subsequent written submissions to the Court, the trustees emphasized their concern about a lack of fairness in the process followed by reason of the lack of, and often belated, disclosure by the SDA both before and after the hearing by the Superintendent, a concern already noted and dealt with in part. I have declined to stay this proceeding, or the proceedings of the Superintendent, or to set those aside on the basis of the trustees' concerns about alleged failure of disclosure.
