Abbott went toward the barrier earlier described which was locked and "the nurse would be on the other side of the barrier giving out the pills". He never made it to the lineup for the pill parade. "I was on my way down when an altercation broke out." He was, however, walking down the strip in a northerly direction and got three-quarters of the way there. He saw the lineup and:
"Q.	there were quite a few people ahead of you?
A.	Yes, somewhere in the vicinity of 15 to 30."
