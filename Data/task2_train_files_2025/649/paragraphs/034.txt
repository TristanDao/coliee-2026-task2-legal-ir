Abbott, at about p. 111 of the transcript, says: "the only reason I said that (hit by first shot) - was because I had birdshot on the bottom of my feet. The first shot got me when I was standing up, so the second must have got me in the bottom of my feet", but later he said he was crawling at the time of the second shot and I am convinced he was probably crawling to get protection just before the second shot got him.
