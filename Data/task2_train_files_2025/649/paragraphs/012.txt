As far as Abbott was concerned, Monday, November 17, 1986, began like any other day and he had followed a routine similar to most days in Collins Bay. He was 21 years old, not particularly well educated and had certification in welding, math and English but in any event no course beyond the grade nine level. He came from a family of five boys. He had earlier been incarcerated at Collins Bay, was released in July of 1986 and "arrived back October of 1986 following 'a break and enter'".
