In his submissions, counsel for the plaintiff argued that the plaintiff was battered and assaulted as the defendant's servants intended to cause to the plaintiff apprehension of imminent harmful or offensive contact and in fact did cause the plaintiff actual harm. Counsel related the assault and battery to both the shooting and the macing of the plaintiff and other inmates. As the plaintiff's pleadings referred only to the assault in terms of the shooting, I will limit myself to comments relating to the shooting.
