There are so many versions of the events of November 17, 1986, one is hard pressed to determine what in fact took place before, during and after that date. This is not surprising, bearing in mind that the incident occurred almost seven years ago and different counsel at different times were retained by the plaintiff and the defendant. Some witnesses, in fact all witnesses, could be excused for not being able to remember incidents or having only a vague recollection of the events. Recognizing that the credibility of the witnesses was of major importance to properly determine the issues here, I listened very carefully to all the witnesses and then ordered and read the transcript which ran to four volumes.
