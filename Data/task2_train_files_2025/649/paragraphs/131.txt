The plaintiff's action against the Crown is based on s. 3 of the
Crown Liability Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. C-50. Paragraph 3(a) provides that:
"3. The Crown is liable in tort for the damages for which, if it were a private person of full age and capacity, it would be liable
(a) in respect of a tort committed by a servant of the Crown;"
Section 2 of the
Crown Liability Act
also provides that agents are servants of the Crown, with the exception of any person appointed or employed by or under the authority of an ordinance of the Yukon Territory or the Northwest Territory.
