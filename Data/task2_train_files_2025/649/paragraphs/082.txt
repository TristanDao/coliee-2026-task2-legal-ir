At 10:30 p.m. Forrest was looking south from the Control Centre to pill parade. Generally that barrier is kept closed but can be opened by the officer at the Control Post. At about 10:40 p.m. he saw a commotion south of the barrier. A broadcast from the Control Post indicated: "a problem at the barrier, that staff were being assaulted by inmates." Another communication came across the radios that weapons should be brought down to the area. 'As weapons were stored in the main gate ... I immediately grabbed a shotgun ... and 4 shells. I also issued a weapon to another officer in the gate - then I left the gate and proceeded to the strip where the incident took place."
