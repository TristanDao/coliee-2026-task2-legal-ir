But the fact is that the visa post, even though it requested "all records concerning your incarceration in Texas" in an April 4, 2005 letter, did not make clear that it wanted a "police clearance form Texas" until July 29, 2008. Had the Applicant and her husband known what was needed, they would have obtained it immediately. The history of the file shows them cooperating and replying promptly.
