At the hearing of this matter, there was indication from Respondent's counsel that everything was in place for a decision to be made and that a decision could be expected in the fairly near future. So I do not think it can be said that, at that stage, it was the Court who secured the decision to issue the visa. That does not mean, of course, that the litigation itself was not necessary to draw attention to the file and that the threat of Court-ordered mandamus was not instrumental in finally resolving what was, really, a non-contentious application for status.
