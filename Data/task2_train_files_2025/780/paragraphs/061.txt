The Applicant has requested a lump sum award of "$4,000.00 or costs awarded based on an hourly rate determined by the Court." This suggests that the Applicant is seeking solicitor-client costs which I do not think can be justified on the facts of this case. I do, however, believe that costs are justified on a party and party basis and that, pursuant to Rule 400(4) of the
Federal Courts Rules
, they should be fixed at $2,000.00.
