The applicant is a citizen of Jamaica. She arrived in Canada in 1984 as a visitor. In 1986, she married Delroy Wynter, and sub­mitted an application for permanent resi­dence in the family class with an undertak­ing of assistance provided by her husband. The applicant was granted a Minister's Permit which allowed her to remain in Canada while her application was being processed. This permit was renewed annually until 1989.
