Layden-Stevenson, J.
: Mr. Kudar is an entrepreneur who ran a seasonal produce wholesaling business in Hungary from 1999-2000. His business was in direct competition with one owned by a local MP, Dr. Gyorgy Atyanski (Atyanski), who has since been convicted of bankruptcy-related charges, but at the time in question, enjoyed immunity from prosecution. Mr. Kudar claims to be a Convention refugee or a person in need of protection. The Refugee Protection Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board (RPD) denied his claim.
