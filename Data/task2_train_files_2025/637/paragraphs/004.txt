Mr. Kudar claimed that the last straw was when Atyanski demanded the return of the buyout money. Mr. Kudar agreed, but was then informed that the amount he owed was double the original amount because he owed interest on the "loan". He did not have the money. Atyanski then suggested a joint illicit business arrangement. Mr. Kudar asked for two weeks to think about it, but instead arranged to flee Hungary for Canada.
