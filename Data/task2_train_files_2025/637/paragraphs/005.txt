The RPD did not make any negative credibility or adverse plausibility findings regarding Mr. Kudar's story. Instead, the board determined that since Atyanski is no longer an MP, and since steps have been taken against him through the judicial system (charged, convicted and sentenced), his influence is greatly reduced. Given the passage of time, there is no more than a mere possibility that Mr. Kudar would face persecution today from Atyanski or his associates. The board reached a similar conclusion with respect to whether Mr. Kudar would face a risk to life, or torture, or cruel and unusual treatment, or punishment.
