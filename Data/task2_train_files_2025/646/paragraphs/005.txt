RSS, a citizen of India, was born on August 17, 1959 in Punjab, India. He is married, with one child. He states that he passed his Matriculation Examination (10th grade) in 1977 and also attended Diploma classes in Mechanical Engineering for four years at the City of Ludhiana, Province of Punjab, India. He worked on a power press, part-time and full-time, from 1974-1980. Since 1980, he operated his own business under the name of "Saggu Enterprises", in the City of Ludhiana, an enterprise involving the manufacture of tools and dyes, plastic mouldings, sheet metal work, and cycle and auto parts.
