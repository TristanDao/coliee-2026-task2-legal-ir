This court has no reason to discount what the visa officer (who, dispassionately has no personal interest in this matter) has sworn from his personal knowledge in his responding affidavit, sworn on August 8, 1993 in Islamabad, Pakistan. It is consistent with his previous deposition, but is fuller in response to the applicant's subsequent affidavit of Amarjit Hunjan, sworn on August 31, 1992. Mr. Hunjan can be the applicant's agent and attorney for purposes of initiating this litigation, but he cannot bind the court to accept the words he has put into RSS, the applicant's mind and mouth through hearsay on this originating motion.
