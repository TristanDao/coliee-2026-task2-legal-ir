The visa officer erred in law and breached the duty of fairness by failing to carry out a full and fair assessment of the applicant and his abilities.
2) He indicated in his application for permanent residence his ability to speak, read and write in the English language. The visa officer failed in his duty to test the applicant's language ability at the time of the personal interview according to the
Immigration Act
and
Regulations
.
3) The visa officer considered irrelevant evidence when assessing the applicant.
