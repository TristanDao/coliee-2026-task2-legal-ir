The applicant with his wife was interviewed by the visa officer at the Canadian High Commission in New Delhi on January 27, 1992, to determine his and their eligibility for landing in Canada. The visa officer announced his decision to the applicant by letter dated February 20, 1992. The applicant by his agent and attorney applied for judicial review on September 2, 1992. On January 4, 1994, the Associate Chief Justice fixed the date and place for the hearing which subsequently occurred.
