On file is a copy of the February 20, 1992 letter containing the decision of the visa officer not to grant permanent residence status to RSS. It is quite short and does not contain much detailed information. It basically indicates that RSS failed to score the minimum number of points necessary, in relation to the factors of age, occupation, experience, education, sponsorship by relative in Canada, language, etc. At the hearing of this application in Winnipeg, the applicant's counsel stated, regarding the visa officer's assessment of points for the factors: age, arranged employment, demographic factor, education, French language, bonus points, and occupational factor-specific vocational preparation (SVP), that they are conceded to be correct. The assessment factors which are in contention herein are: experience, English language, personal suitability, and also whether RSS "was suitable for another occupation" in the visa officer's scope of consideration.
