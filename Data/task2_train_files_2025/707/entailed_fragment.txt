Mr. Justice McKeown, following a decision of Madam Justice Simpson in
Langard v. National Parole Board
(1993), 70 F.T.R. 140 (T.D.), held that for a sentence imposed before November 1, 1992 the provisions of the
Parole Act
applied, and for a sentence imposed after that date, when the
Corrections and Conditional Release Act
came into force, that
Act
was applicable.
