The respondent submits that this formula ignores s. 139(1) of the new
Act
. In my view, s. 139(1) is not a transitional section such as s. 225 is. It governs only situations where multiple sentences are imposed after the coming into force of the new
Act
. Although it is not necessary for these Reasons, in my view, even if s. 139(1) were applicable, it states that the term of imprisonment commences "at the beginning of the first of those sentences to be served". In the case at bar, that sentence commenced at a time when the
Parole Act
was in force, and accordingly it should govern, and not the new
Act
.
