I am not persuaded that in this case any basis is established for an award of costs on a solicitor and client basis, even if a litigant, who is not a lawyer, acting for herself and her corporation, may cause more than ordinary costs for the defendant, Her Majesty the Queen. In the circumstances, I award costs to the defendant, on the usual party and party basis, unless by agreement the parties settle the matter of costs on some other basis.
Action dismissed.
