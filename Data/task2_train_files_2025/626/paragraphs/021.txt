Mr. Michael Schwantes, then a special investigator with Revenue Canada in Toronto, was directed to serve a Letter of Requirement on Olympia, which he tried to do on November 26, 1985. Ms. David, upon whom he served the Letter, refused to accept it, a fact noted by Schwantes on the face of the original. The Letter was in fact served, if not accepted. It directed that Olympia produce by December 12, 1985, any book, record, document or information relating to transactions of Olympia during the period October 1, 1980 to September 30, 1985, and it listed various classes of financial records and correspondence which were directed to be supplied. Ms. David apparently indicated that books and records could not be produced because some were in Calgary for purposes of litigation there. There was no compliance by Olympia with the Letter of Requirement.
