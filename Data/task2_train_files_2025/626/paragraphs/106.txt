To establish a violation of s. 15 the plaintiff Ms. David must establish that a distinction is made based on a personal characteristic which results in denial to the plaintiff of one of the four basic equality rights protected by s. 15(1), that is, equality before the law, equality under the law, equal protection of the law and equal benefit of the law. The focus at this stage is whether the law has distinguished between the claimant and others based on personal characteristics. Secondly, the plaintiff must establish that the denial of the right results in discrimination on one of the prohibited grounds or an analogous ground. [see footnote 39]
