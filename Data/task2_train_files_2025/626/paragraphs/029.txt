On July 16, 1986 a Notice of Assessment was issued to Olympia including a net assessment of taxes unpaid of $64,948, plus interest and penalties for a total amount unpaid in excess of $102,000. Ms. David later telephoned Mr. Martin, and she confirmed by letter of September 12, 1986 to another officer of the department, that she acknowledged that $54,057 was unpaid taxes, but she disputed assessment of interest and penalties. In response to that letter Mr. Kluger, the Regional Director wrote to remind Olympia that it had 90 days from the date of the assessment to file a Notice of Objection. No such notice was filed at any time.
