In the 1970's Olympia apparently had the benefit of a special deduction rate worked out by the Excise branch with Olympia, or of a rate of 10% applicable in the case of manufacturers of drapes that sold the product including installation costs. The evidence is not clear [see footnote 4] about the rate or the arrangements but there is no dispute that Olympia had enjoyed the benefit of such a deduction rate in the 1970's and early 1980's.
