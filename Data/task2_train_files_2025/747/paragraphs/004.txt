Counsel for the plaintiff did raise one matter but did not press it in argument. The issue is whether the Prothonotary erred in accepting affidavit evidence consisting of: (1) an October 15, 1998 decision by the Department of Veterans Affairs awarding the defendant a disability pension on account of post-traumatic stress disorder related to the performance of peacekeeping duties with the Canadian Armed Forces, and (2) a December 6, 1999 decision by the Veterans Review and Appeal Board increasing that pension to 45%. Counsel for the plaintiff asked me not to base my decision on this point. As a result, I consider this ground to have been abandoned.
