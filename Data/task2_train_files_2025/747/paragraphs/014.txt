Sergeant Duplessis claims that in February 1997, his duties and responsibilities were unilaterally altered. He was demoted and all of his previous supervisory responsibilities removed. The decision was made on the determination he was disabled and unfit to perform his duties. Sergeant Duplessis claims the decision was made in bad faith and states the reasons given for his release were a pretext to get rid of him. He alleges he was improperly evaluated and ultimately improperly scheduled for release by the Armed Forces.
