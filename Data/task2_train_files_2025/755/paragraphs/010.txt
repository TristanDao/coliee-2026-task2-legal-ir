The standard of review for decisions rendered by an adjudicator appointed under s. 242(1) of the
Code
is that of correctness when the question concerns the jurisdiction of the adjudicator. However, if the question is one of fact and law which is within the tribunal's powers, the standard of review is that of the patently unreasonable decision (
Lamontagne v. 2747-7173 Québec Inc.
, [2000] F.T.R. Uned. 896; [2000] F.C.J. No. 2063 (T.D.)).
