I find no merit in this submission. First, s. 349 of the
Act respecting industrial accidents and occupational diseases
is explicitly restricted to examining and deciding any question contemplated in the
Act
. The
Act
gives an employee who is the victim of an occupational disease or industrial accident the right to receive compensation as a result. The
Act
does not deal at all with the issue of whether the dismissal of an employee was unjust, which, in this case, is governed by ss. 240 and 242 of the
Code
.
