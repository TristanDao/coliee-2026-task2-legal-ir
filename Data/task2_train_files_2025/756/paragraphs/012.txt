The PRRA officer does not play a role in the submission of evidence. If the evidence is insufficient, the applicant must bear the consequences, and the officer has no obligation to inform the applicant of this (Selliah, above, at paragraph 22; see also Youssef v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration), 2006 FC 864, [2006] F.C.J. No. 1101 (QL) at paragraph 33).
