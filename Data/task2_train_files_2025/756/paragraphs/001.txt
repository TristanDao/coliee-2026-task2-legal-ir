[Translation]: This is an application for judicial review of a decision by a Canada Border Services Agency (CBSA) officer rejecting the applicant’s application for pre-removal risk assessment (PRRA) on the ground that the applicant was not considered to be a person who would be subjected to a personal risk, a risk to his life, a risk of torture or cruel and unusual punishment under sections 96 and 97 of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act, S.C. 2001, c. 27 (the Act) if he were returned to Romania.
