In the context of a PRRA application, the burden of proof is on the person wishing to be granted refugee status under subsection 114(1) of the IRPA (Hailu v. Canada (Solicitor General), 2005 FC 229, [2005] F.C.J. No. 268 at paragraphs 18 and 22). The onus is on this person to demonstrate that they should be granted refugee status.
