In his October 26, 2006 response to the Compliance Order, Mr. Camplin continued to interpret it as not applying to him as a natural person and was not intended to “compel me to provide my private information and /or documents held by me in my private capacity as a “natural person” for my own benefit.” He therefore only provided information while acting in his capacity of “legal representative” of the taxpayer for its benefit.
