I agree entirely with Justice von Finckenstein the ITA does not carve out a distinction, for the purposes of an RFI or a Compliance Order, between a natural person and the legal representative of the taxpayer. It is a distinction which is meaningless and without a difference. Justice Von Finckenstein said so to Mr. Camplin, who despite clear words in the Compliance Order, knowingly and deliberately failed to comply with its clear and unambiguous terms that as a human being Mr. Camplin was required to provide information and documents on all of his assets specified in the RFI.
