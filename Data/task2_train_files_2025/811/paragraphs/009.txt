On March 2, 2007, Transport Canada issued a direction to Air Canada. The direction stated that two provisions of the
Code
were contravened: subsection 125(1)(c), which relates to the employer's obligation to investigate the incident and subsection 135.1(8)(b), which relates to the designation of members of the WPC to perform the functions of the WPC. Air Canada was directed to terminate these contraventions.
