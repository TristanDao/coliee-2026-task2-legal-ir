In August and September of 2001, without consulting anyone, Mr. Han arranged the release of 159 Falun Gong practitioners. After returning from a trip to the United States in September of the same year, he suspected that he was under surveillance and he left for Canada a few days later. Mr. Han arrived in Canada on September 16, 2001, and made his refugee claim on December 10, 2002.
