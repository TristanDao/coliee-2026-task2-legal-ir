No authority is cited in support of the proposition advanced. In Xie v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration), [2005] F.C.R. 305; (2004), 243 D.L.R. (4th) 385 (C.A.) (Xie), the Federal Court of Appeal held that the RPD must decide the issue regarding a claimant's exclusion, from refugee protection, before dealing with the merits of the claim. Once the board finds that a claimant is excluded from refugee protection, there is nothing more that it can or should do.
