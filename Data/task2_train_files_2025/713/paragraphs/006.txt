The RPD found widespread and systemic human rights abuses in Chinabetween 1982 and 2001 (the years during which Mr. Han was a member of the Chinese administration). Although it focused largely on evidence of abuse directed toward Falun Gong practitioners, it noted that this abuse was not exclusively directed toward that group. Documentary evidence from Amnesty International and Human Rights Watch supported findings that numerous prisoners and detainees have died as a result of torture and abuse by the Chinese authorities. The United States Department of State Country Report on Human Rights Practices for 2003 reported the use of torture by members of the police and security apparatus.
