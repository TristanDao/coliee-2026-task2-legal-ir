The RPD concluded that there exist serious reasons for believing that Mr. Han is a person described in Article 1, section F(a) of the Refugee Convention and excluded him under section 98 of the IRPA. It did not accept Mr. Han's evidence regarding conditions in prisons and labour camps under his control. It concluded, on a balance of probabilities, that he was aware of the abuses that were occurring. The board found that Mr. Han's testimony lacked credibility due to internal inconsistencies. This finding was specifically made in relation to testimony about the conditions in labour camps under his control.
