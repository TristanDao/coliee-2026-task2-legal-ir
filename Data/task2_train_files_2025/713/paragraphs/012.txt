The second issue is Mr. Han's reliance, in his written submissions, on the reasons of Mr. Justice Blanchard in Thamotharem v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) 2006 FC 16 in support of his argument that the application of Guideline 7 (reverse order questioning), "compounded with the special nature of the hearing, negatively impacted on the claim". He contends that the RPD, apparently influenced by the manner in which the order of questioning had been set out in the analysis of exclusion, used the same system in the examination of "inclusion".
