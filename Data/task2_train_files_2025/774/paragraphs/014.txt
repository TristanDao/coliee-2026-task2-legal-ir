On issues of fettering discretion, the Federal
Court of Appeal has held that while fettering of discretion used to be

fettered discretion is per se unreasonable (Stemijon Investments Ltd.
v Canada (Attorney General), 2011 FCA 299 at paras 23-24 [Stemijon];
Gordon v Canada (Attorney General), 2016 FC 643 at paras 25-28).
