The IAD reasonably concluded that s.168 should
be applied in this case without a show cause hearing. The IAD had indications
that the Applicant did not have a clear intention to pursue his claim with a
consistent pattern of non-response. The Applicant’s lawyer could not contact him,
and subsequently recused himself for this reason. He did not appear at his
reconsideration hearing. No response was received to a request for a written
statement of compliance. Critically, the Applicant did not notify the IAD of
two separate address changes.
