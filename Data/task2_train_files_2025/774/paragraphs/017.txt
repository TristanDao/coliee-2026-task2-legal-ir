The Applicant argues that the IAD should have
taken into account other provisions of the IRPA instead of focusing solely on
s.168 of the IRPA. The Applicant argues that s.164 of the IRPA allows for an
abandonment hearing to be held in absence of an applicant. The Applicant
further argues that s.67 of the IRPA allows for an appeal to be allowed or
removal stayed without the presence of the applicant. Further, the Applicant
points to s.175 which allows the IAD to base its conclusions on “credible and trustworthy evidence.”
