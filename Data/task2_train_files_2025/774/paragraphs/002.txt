On January 25, 2013, the Immigration Appeal
Division [IAD] and Canada Border Services Agency [CBSA] agreed to a stay of the
Applicant’s removal from Canada for a four year period subject to various terms
and conditions. In addition to bi-yearly reporting obligations, the Applicant
was also required to inform the CBSA and the IAD in writing of any change in
address. This obligation rested with him, as per the terms of the stay order, for
a period of four years (ending with a reconsideration on January 25, 2017).
