The applicant informed the respondent that it was acting illegally by waiving compulsory pilotage in the circumstances, and that it would be bringing legal proceedings to stop this illegal action. Accordingly, on September 20, 1993, the respondent filed its action for declaratory judgment seeking a declaration that it was entitled, in fact and in law, under s. 5 of the
Regulations
