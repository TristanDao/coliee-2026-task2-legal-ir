This brings me to the next point. There is no question of legal interpretation in issue. The issue is rather one of interpreting facts. Giving declaratory judgment in the present case would amount to ruling on the question of fact as to the availability of pilots. On reading the
Regulations
, the situations in which the requirement of having a pilot on the ship may be waived are clear. Whether the Authority has used the
Regulations
properly is a question of fact which should be determined at a trial and not by declaratory judgment.
