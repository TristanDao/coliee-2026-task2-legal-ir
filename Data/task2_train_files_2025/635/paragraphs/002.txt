The plaintiff (respondent) is a federal Crown corporation established under the
Pilotage Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. P-14 (the
Act
) to administer in the interests of safety an efficient pilotage service within the regions set out in the
Act
. The Authority is established under s. 3(1) of the
Act
:
"3(1) Each Pilotage Authority named in the schedule is hereby established as a body corporate consisting of a Chairman and not more than six other members."
