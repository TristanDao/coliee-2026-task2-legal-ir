Therefore, a cause of action based on s. 22(1) applies only in the case of a registered trademark. In the case before me, the trademark is an unregistered one and, as such, the plaintiff has no cause of action for depreciation of goodwill. As the plaintiff 's cause of action is one of passing off, the plaintiff must therefore rely on the unfair competition section, namely s. 7, of the
