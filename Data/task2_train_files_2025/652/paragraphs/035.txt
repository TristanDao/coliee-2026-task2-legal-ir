In paragraph 14 of the Amended Statement of Claim, the plaintiff claims:
"The defendants by virtue of their activities in association with the trademark 'Copper' for brewed alcoholic beverages have passed off and directed public attention to their wares in such a way as to be likely to cause confusion in Canada between the wares and business of the plaintiff and the wares and business of the defendants at the time the defendants commenced so to direct attention to their wares in association with 'Copper'. The defendants are liable to the plaintiff for all such unlawful activities."
