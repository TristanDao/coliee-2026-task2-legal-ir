This dispute concerns the shape of the respondent's HID-A-BAG refuse container. The container in question features, in words of its creator Mr. Dennis Neufeldt, "an upwardly inclined rectangular shape, departing from the vertical by approximately 30E to 35E, with a vertically truncated back end and a forwardly extending horizontal base." HID-A-BAG containers are available in a single or double bin version and may be equipped with "animal-proof" latches, a recycling top or a combination waste/ recycling lid. The double bin version also features a latched unloading door at the rear for easy removal of garbage bags.
