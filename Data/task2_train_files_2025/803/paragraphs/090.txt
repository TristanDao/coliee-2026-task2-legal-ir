To summarize, the plaintiffs are asking this Court not to grant a stay of execution of the judgment for three reasons: (1) both parties contracted on the basis that the freight would be paid without deduction, (2) the plaintiffs are out of pocket for the freight to the actual carrier, as well as for the taxes and duties paid to Customs, and (3) the defendant specifically agreed in the insurance settlement not to raise these counterclaims as a reason to refuse payment if credit was re-instated.
