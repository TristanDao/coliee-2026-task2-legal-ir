Teitelbaum, J.
: This is a motion for summary judgment brought by the plaintiffs and a cross-motion by the defendants. The plaintiffs, Pantainer Ltd. and Panalpina Inc. (plaintiffs) bring this motion for summary judgment to recover amounts owing by the defendant for freight ($134,513.40) and ancillary charges totalling $144,037.98 plus interest. The defendant claims that damage to cargos and financial losses attributed to alleged breaches of contract by the plaintiffs give it a right to set-off against the claim.
