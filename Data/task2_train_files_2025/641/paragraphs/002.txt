The Applicant, Marian Kamara, a citizen of Sierra Leone, is 39 years of age and a refugee claimant who fears persecution or a risk to her life in Sierra Leone by the brother of her deceased husband. She alleges that her brother-in-law, an ex-Rebel and current member of the Sierra Leonean army, insisted on marrying her, and threatened her life should she fail to comply with his proposal.
