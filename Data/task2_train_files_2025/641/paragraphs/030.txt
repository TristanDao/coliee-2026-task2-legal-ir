The adverse inference drawn by the Board in respect to the allegedly contradictory statements by the Applicant concerning the whereabouts and status of her brother-in-law in 2000 is also suspect. While the integration of the rebels in the Sierra Leonean army occurred at the early part of 2000, it does not necessarily follow that every rebel was integrated at the same time. He could well have been in a refugee camp for part of the year.
