Counsel may serve and file submissions with respect to certification of a question of general importance, if any, within ten (10) days of the date of these reasons. Each party will have a further four (4) days to serve and file a reply, if any, to the submissions of the opposite party. Following consideration of those submissions, an order will issue dismissing the application for judicial review and disposing of the issue of a serious question of general importance as contemplated by section 74(d) of the IRPA.
