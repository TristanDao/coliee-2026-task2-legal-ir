Mr. Liban argues that the officer undertook to convene a hearing. Accordingly, he had a legitimate expectation that a hearing would be held before his risk assessment was completed. In their respective affidavits, the officer and Mr. Liban's previous counsel express different recollections of their communications on this subject. It seems to me that the officer, at least, gave the impression that he would be holding a hearing but this is not enough to create a legitimate expectation.
