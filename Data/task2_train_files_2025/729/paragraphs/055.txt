The applicants submit that the officer focused on the risk aspect of the humanitarian and compassionate application. They further submit that, while the officer mentioned the appropriate test (the existence of unusual, undeserved or disproportionate hardship), the officer applied the wrong test. This is said to be evidenced by the fact that the officer relied upon the existence of state protection and changed country conditions. The officer ought to have instead considered the issue of hardship.
