It is instructive to review the applicants' submissions in their humanitarian and compassionate application regarding the issue of risk. The applicants:
- attached their Personal Information Form narrative and stated that the risk they faced was described in the narrative;
- attached country condition documentation;
- stated that "in assessing the risk that the applicant's [sic] face if returned to their home countries it is useful to perform an assessment of the usual issues identified at refugee hearings" and went on to discuss the agents of persecution, nexus, state protection, and the existence of an internal flight alternative; and
- did not discuss unusual, undeserved or disproportionate hardship.
