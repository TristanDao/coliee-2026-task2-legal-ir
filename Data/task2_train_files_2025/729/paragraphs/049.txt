In my view, on these facts, the applicants have failed to establish that they required disclosure of "Citizenship Laws of the World" in order to present their case fully and fairly to the officer. They had raised the issue of the family's ability to reintegrate into either Ecuador or Venezuela because some members of the family were not citizens of those countries. They were aware of the existence of the impugned document a year before the decision was made. The applicants could have filed supplementary submissions if they wished to contest the information found in the document.
