Finally, the Defendants argue that the real allegation of the Plaintiffs is that the Wheat Board acted "contrary to the statutory directive", or that its actions are ultra vires. The Defendants contend that if there were any factual foundation for such a claim, it is not a proper matter for an action but rather an allegation that should proceed by way of an application for judicial review to determine if the actions complained about are in fact ultra vires the Wheat Board. In essence, the Defendants are arguing that the Plaintiffs are seeking declaratory relief, a remedy that can only be sought by way of an application for judicial review.
