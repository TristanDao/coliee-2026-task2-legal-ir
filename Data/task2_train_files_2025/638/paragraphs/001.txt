[Translation]: In a decision dated January 12, 2006, the Canadian Human Rights Commission (the Commission) dismissed a complaint made by Paul Ouellet against his employer, the Department of Human Resources and Social Development Canada. Mr. Ouellet alleged being a victim of discrimination because of his disability, contrary to section 7 of the
Canadian Human Rights Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. H-6 (the Act).
