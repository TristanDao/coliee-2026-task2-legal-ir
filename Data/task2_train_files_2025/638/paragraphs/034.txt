With respect, I cannot accept this argument. When an investigation is conducted, all the relevant evidence must be considered. In the case at bar, I am of the opinion that the investigator complied with this requirement. She read all of Dr. Frigault's letters and restated their contents in her report, even citing extensive excerpts. Accordingly, the Commission was well aware of Dr. Frigault's position and was therefore in a position to render an enlightened decision on this point.
