What is the situation in this case? The applicant claims that the fact that Dr. Frigault was not questioned is a major omission that vitiates the results of the investigation and, consequently, the Commission's decision which is challenged herein. Not only did he conduct an assessment of Mr. Ouellet and give him psychotherapy, he also outlined a return-to-work plan and attended the meeting on August 27, 2003, the goal of which was to plan Mr. Ouellet's return to work. Accordingly, he was the person in the best position to explain how the employer's approach did not meet Mr. Ouellet's needs, if we accept his lawyer's submissions.
