On October 6, 2004, the Commission forwarded Mr. Ouellet's complaint to representatives of the Department to obtain their reply. On January 11, 2005, the reply was forwarded to the investigator appointed by the Commission. Meanwhile, in December 2004, the Commission appointed Sylvie McNicoll to investigate Mr. Ouellet's complaint. However, it was investigator Anick Hébert who took over in June 2005 and wrote the investigation report dated September 19, 2005, after completing the research and analysing the information.
