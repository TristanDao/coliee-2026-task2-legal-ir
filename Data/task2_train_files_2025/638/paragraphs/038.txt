The applicant also criticized the investigator for not having examined the main issue of his complaint, that is to say, the matter of whether the Department had properly accommodated the applicant by preparing a return-to-work plan that met his needs. In support of his argument, he submits that the investigation record did not contain any document showing the Department had prepared a return-to-work plan and that the investigator did not do a thorough job, having failed to question the employer's witnesses about their return-to-work plan. In addition, the applicant states that the investigation report did not contain any analysis of the employer's duty to accommodate or of the return-to-work plan proposed by the employer.
