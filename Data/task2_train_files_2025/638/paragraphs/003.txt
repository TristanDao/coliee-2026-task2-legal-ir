In submitting this application for judicial review, Mr. Ouellet is asking that the Commission's decision be quashed on the ground that the investigation of his complaint was not thorough. More specifically, he alleges that the investigator did not question a key witness he identified and that she did not conduct any analysis of the employer's duty to accommodate. In addition, Mr. Ouellet is asking this Court to return his complaint to the Commission with an order that it be dealt with according to the reasons specified in the decision to be rendered.
