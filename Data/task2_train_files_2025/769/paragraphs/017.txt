The applicants urge me to follow
Vic Restaurant Inc. v. Montreal (City)
, [1959] S.C.R. 58, on the grounds that the facts before me are similar. However in that case the restaurant that sought renewal of permits to sell liquor and operate a restaurant had been sold and therefore no mandamus for licence could be given. There were outstanding prosecutions against the applicants for violations of the municipal bylaw which was the subject of legal challenge. The difference in the case before me is that no one can foresee the nature of a new MAI. Some of the same legal issues might arise in any future MAI but there also may be new legal issues.
