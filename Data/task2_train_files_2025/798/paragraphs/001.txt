Reed, J.
: This is an application by the Minister to have a decision of the Convention Refugee Determination Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board (the "Board") set aside. The Board found the respondent (hereinafter "the claimant") to be a convention refugee and did not find he was excluded from protection because of his involvement with the Sazeman-e Mojahedin-e Khalq-e Iran ("Mujahideen"). The Minister had argued before the Board that the claimant, as a result of his involvement with that organization, should be excluded from protection pursuant to Article 1F(a) of the
United Nations Convention Relating to the Status of Refugees
(the "
Convention
").
