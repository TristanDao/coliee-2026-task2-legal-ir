In relation to the test, Lamer, C.J., confirmed that the "classic test" in
Committee for Justice and Liberty v. National Energy Board
, supra, applies to allegations of a reasonable apprehension of bias involving concerns related to the institutional independence of an administrative tribunal. He also noted, at page 50, "... that the grounds for the apprehension must be 'substantial', and that the test for bias should be applied flexibly in the context of administrative tribunals".
