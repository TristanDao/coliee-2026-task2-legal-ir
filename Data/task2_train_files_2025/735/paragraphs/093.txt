In his outline of the principles to be applied in analysing the s. 11(d)
Charter
right, Lamer, C.J., reiterated the essential conditions of independence outlined in
R. v. Valente
, supra, and emphasized that the court adopted those conditions recognizing that they could be applied flexibly in the context of a variety of tribunals. However, he cautioned at page 286 that "[a]lthough the conditions are susceptible to flexible application in order to suit the needs of different tribunals, the essence of each condition must be protected in every case".
