The facts pertaining to this application are quite straightforward and not in dispute. The plaintiff was incorporated in the U.S. state of Delaware, where it has its head office, and is registered in British Columbia as an extra-provincial company. It has been doing business in Canada since 1975 and leases a warehouse in Kelowna, British Columbia, where it has assets of over $150,000 in accounts receivable worth between $200,000 and $500,000 in the ordinary course of its business.
