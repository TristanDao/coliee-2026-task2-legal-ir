This originating notice of motion is in response to a notice of allegation sent by Apotex on June 12, 1995 which alleges the invalidity of the '334 patent:
"Re:
CIPROFLOXACIN HYDROCHLO­RIDE TABLETS 100 MG, 250 MG, 500 MG AND 750 MG.
This is a Notice of Allegation pursuant to Section 5(3)(b) of the Patented Medicines (Notice of Compliance) Regulations.
We allege that patent 1,322,334 is not valid. The factual and legal basis is as follows.
Patent 1,322,334 discloses no invention beyond what is claimed in patent 1,218,067. More particularly, there is nothing further inventive in mixing cipro­floxacin hydrochloride with a pharmaceu­tically acceptable diluent or carrier to make a composition or the use of such composi­tion."
