The application for the '334 patent was filed after the revocation of s. 41(1). For Bayer, this is important because of its view that the case law relied upon by Apotex is based upon s. 41(1) and is there­fore not directly applicable to this proceed­ing. For Apotex, the case law regarding the validity of pharmaceutical composition claims remained the same both when s. 41(1) was in force and after its revoca­tion. In the view of Apotex, therefore, the out­come of this application should not be affected in any manner by s. 41(1) or its revocation.
