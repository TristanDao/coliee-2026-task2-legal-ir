The respondents further argue that the applicant has recycled several of its unsuccessful arguments in
Bell Canada v. CEP
[see footnote 7] in its submissions before this Court. However, I do not consider it necessary and even appropriate to dispose of any other issue in this matter, given my conclusion that the application for judicial review is premature. Consequently, I do not intend to undertake the judicial review sought by the applicant. In the words of my colleague Gibson, J., in
Novopharm Ltd. v. Aktiebolaget Astra et al.
(1996), 110 F.T.R. 307 (T.D.), "[t]o do so would effectively be to engage in the analysis that another judge of this Court might at a later date be required to take".
