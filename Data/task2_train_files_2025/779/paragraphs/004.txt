As part of the procedure to market beclomethasone dipropionate inhalers, the respondent served on the applicants a notice of allegation and detailed statement of fact and law, in which it alleged, pursuant to s. 5(1)(b)(iv) of the
Regulations
, that no claim for the medicine itself nor for its use would be infringed by the making, constructing, using or selling of the proposed generic inhalers. Infringement is impossible since the applicants' patents do not contain any claims for the medicine beclomethasone dipropionate itself. They solely describe devices for administering the medicament.
