The central question in this application is whether the Tribunal had jurisdiction to act. This is a question of Law. The Supreme Court of Canada in
Pushpanathan v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, [1998] 1 S.C.R. 982, addendum [1998] 1 S.C.R. 1222; 226 N.R. 201, decided that the standard of review applicable to a decision rendered by the Immigration and Refugee Board, Appeal Division on a question of law is correctness. I am bound by that decision and will apply the correctness standard in reviewing the Board's decision.
