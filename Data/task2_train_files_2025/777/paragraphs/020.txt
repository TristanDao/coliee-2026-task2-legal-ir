The Federal Court of Appeal also dealt with the Appeal Division's jurisdiction in
Minister of Employment and Immigration v. Selby
, [1981] 1 F.C. 273; 110 D.L.R.(3d) 126 (F.C.A.). In all three decisions, the Court essentially decided that the Appeal Division could not allow an appeal unless the appeal was made by a person entitled by law to appeal before the Board, namely a permanent resident.
