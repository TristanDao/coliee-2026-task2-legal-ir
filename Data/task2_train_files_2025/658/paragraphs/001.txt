Harrington, J.
: Turk, Kurd, Islamist, intellectual, human rights activist, pacifist and draft dodger; Erkan Ates is all these things. But a refugee he is not. This is the judicial review of the decision of a member of the Refugee Protection Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board dated December 15, 2003, in which it was held that Mr. Ates is neither a refugee nor a person otherwise in need of international protection within the meaning of sections 96 and 97 of the
Immigration and Refugee Protection Act
, S.C. 2001, c. 27.
