At this stage of the process, the Court's role is limited to verifying whether the RPD's inferences and conclusions about the lack of credible evidence to support the applicant's claim are reasonable or not. The reasons given here by the RPD are not to be read hypercritically by the Court nor is this administrative tribunal required to refer to every piece of evidence received (
Cepeda-Gutierrez v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, [1998] F.C.J. No. 1425 ).
