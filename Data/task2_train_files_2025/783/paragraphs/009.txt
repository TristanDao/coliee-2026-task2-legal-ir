A careful reading of the transcript of the hearing does not lead the Court to believe that the decision was made in a capricious manner or without regard to the material before it. On the contrary, the RPD exposed sufficient reason to find the applicant not credible and to justify why it reached its negative conclusion. The RPD found many contradictions and also found implausible the communication between the applicant and his boss about reference letters and the inspection of the underground tank, while fearing for his life from his boss.
