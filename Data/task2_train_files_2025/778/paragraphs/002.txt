Ms. Lin claims that she was introduced to Christianity in February 2001 and began to attend church regularly in March of that year. Following the arrest of two members of the church in October of 2002, she was advised to go into hiding. While in hiding, she learned that the Public Service Bureau (PSB) was accusing her of being involved in illegal religious activities and was trying to locate her. The PSB allegedly searched her house and ordered her to surrender. Fearing arrest, she fled the country.
