The respondent submits that the visa officer reasonably concluded that the applicant had failed to discharge his burden, pursuant to section 8(1) of the former
Immigration Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. I-2 (the "former
Act
"), of satisfying her that he met the legislative requirements for admission to Canada. Specifically, the visa officer was not satisfied that he had the NOC-defined employment experience as a computer programmer or systems analyst. The respondent argues that the applicant's submissions amount to a disagreement with the visa officer's conclusions, however, this is not a ground to find a reviewable error on judicial review.
