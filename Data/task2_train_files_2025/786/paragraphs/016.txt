The respondent refers to the deferential standard of review of visa officer decisions with respect to applications for permanent residence, set out in
To v. Minister of Employment and Immigration
, [1996] F.C.J. No. 696 (F.C.A.), namely the same standard established in
Maple Lodge Farms Ltd. v. Canada and Canada (Minister of Economic Development)
, [1982] 2 S.C.R. 2; 44 N.R. 354. The respondent also relies on the more recent case law of
Liu (Y.) v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
(2001), 208 F.T.R. 99 (T.D.), where this court has described this standard as equating to the reasonableness simpliciter standard.
