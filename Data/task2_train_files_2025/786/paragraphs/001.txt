Mosley, J.
: This is an application for judicial review of the decision of visa officer Moira Escott (the "visa officer"), dated June 17, 2002, denying Mr. Pandi Rukmangathan's application for permanent residence in Canada. Both the applicant and the visa officer have filed affidavits in these proceedings concerning what transpired at an interview held at Detroit, Michigan on May 21, 2002. There is a clear conflict between their evidence. For the reasons outlined below, I prefer the evidence of the applicant and find that he was denied procedural fairness.
