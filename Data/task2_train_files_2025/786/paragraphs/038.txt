In my opinion, the visa officer breached the duty of fairness by failing to apprise the applicant of her concerns related to these diplomas. The applicant was aware that evidence of his educational background was required in order to satisfy his onus of proof. He provided such evidence. The visa officer's problems with two of his diplomas, in that they do not contain his full name, but rather state "R. Pandi", ignores the fact that all his educational certificates from India, documents relating to his property in India, his curriculum vitae, even his own signature on his marriage certificate, refer to him as "R. Pandi". This factual concern could have easily been addressed if the applicant had been apprised of it, however, I am persuaded that he was not afforded such opportunity.
