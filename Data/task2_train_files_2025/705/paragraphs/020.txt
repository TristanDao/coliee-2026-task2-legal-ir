The Officer assigned only "the slightest weight" to an affidavit from a Canadian citizen originally from Sierra Leone, Ahmed Kabba, attesting that he recalled the Applicant's family in Blama, as he was from a nearby village and had family in Blama. The Officer observed that Mr. Kabba did not reside in Blama, did not meet the Applicant there, and provided very few details other than vaguely recalling having a discussion with the Applicant's brother, who would have only been four or five years-old when Mr. Kabba left the country. He also did not indicate whether his family still lived there or whether he or the Applicant had attempted to contact them.
