The Applicants, a husband and wife, their daughter and the wife's two sons are citizens of Peru. The principal Applicant, Raul Ezeta Ruiz, claims a well-founded fear of persecution on account of his political opinion and membership in the Peruvian Workers' Union (the "Union"). He testified that his involvement in the Union led to his arrest, detention and torture by the Peruvian police. His wife and children based their claims on the same facts alleged by the principal Applicant.
