Gibson, J.
: These reasons relate to an application for review of a decision of an immigration officer, notice of which was conveyed to the applicant by letter dated the 14th day of January, 1994 signed by the acting area manager of the Canada Immigration Centre in Ottawa, whereby the immigration officer denied the applicant's request to exempt her from the visa requirements of s. 9(1) of the
Immigration Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. I-2 pursuant to an application made by the applicant under s. 114(2) of that
Act
. The application was made on the 27th day of September, 1993.
