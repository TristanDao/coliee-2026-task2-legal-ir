By telex received from the Canadian Mission in Tehran on the 5th of November, 1993, the immigration officer was advised of a full page newspaper article from the Tehran Times of the 21st of October of the same year which discussed an in-vitro fertilization program at a hospital in Tehran. The program had been operating for three years. The article suggested that money being spent by the Iranian government for in-vitro fertilization abroad might better be spent expanding the facilities in Iran. The immigration officer concluded on the basis of the telex that in-vitro fertilization is neither illegal nor immoral in Iran as alleged by the applicant.
