Barnes, J.
: This is an application for judicial review of a decision of the Immigration and Refugee Board (Board) rendered on February 3, 2005. In that decision, the Applicant's claim to be a Convention Refugee was rejected on the ground that she had not produced sufficient credible evidence to establish her identity. In the result, the Board found it unnecessary to consider whether her claim for refugee protection as a person facing persecution or as a person otherwise in need of protection was well founded.
