The Applicant arrived in Canada on June 25, 2003 carrying a false Korean passport. She claimed to have been born in China and asserted that she was a national of the People's Republic of China, albeit of Korean ethnicity. In the Applicant's Personal Information Form (PIF), she indicated that she had left China for South Korea by air on June 24, 2003 and then departed by air from South Korea to Canada the following day.
