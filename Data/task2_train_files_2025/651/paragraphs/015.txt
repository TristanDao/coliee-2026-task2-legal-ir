As a result of the Supreme Court's decision in
Dunsmuir
v.
New-Brunswick
, 2008 SCC 9, I believe the reasonableness standard is applicable to review questions of fact and credibility. On the other hand, there is no need to apply a standard of review analysis to the argument that the applicants did not receive a fair hearing: if there has been a breach of procedural fairness, this Court must intervene (
Sketchley
v.
Canada (Attorney General)
, 2005 FCA 404).
