Both wares are wine. "TRIUS" is a Canadian wine and "TRIO", a Chilean wine. "TRIUS" wines are sold only in Ontario. "TRIO" wines are sold across Canada. In Ontario, "TRIO" wine is sold exclusively in VINTAGES stores or VINTAGES sections of LCBO stores. Conversely, "TRIUS" wine is sold either in Andrés' own stores (where "TRIUS" wines are not available) or commonly available in sections of the LCBO stores dedicated to Ontario wines.
