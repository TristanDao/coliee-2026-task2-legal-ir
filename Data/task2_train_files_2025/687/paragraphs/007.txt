On the other hand, Vina alleges that Andrés has not provided any evidence of any consumers being confused, its evidence fails to even suggest that there is a likelihood of such confusion, and has taken no step to enjoin Vina from using its "TRIO" trademark in association with wines: consequently, the "TRIO" registration in no way affects Andrés' rights with respect to its use of the "TRIUS" label design in the market place.
