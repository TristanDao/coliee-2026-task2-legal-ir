Although
Ahani v. Canada
, supra, involved an application of interpretative principles in the context of a constitutional challenge to the validity of a statutory provision, the question arises as to whether the fundamental principles enunciated in that case apply in determining the nature of reference proceedings under s. 18 of the
Citizenship Act
. In order to answer that question, the purpose and import of the
Immigration Act
and the
Citizenship Act
must be considered.
