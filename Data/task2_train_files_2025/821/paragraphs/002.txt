On the first day of hearings, the ap­plicant asked that, due to his disabilities, he be allowed to make further written sub­missions in lieu of oral representations. Counsel for the respondent eventually ac­cepted his new submissions for the most part, although he asked the court to make a determination in respect of certain portions to which he maintained an objection. Be­cause of the circumstances, both parties agreed that it would be appropriate for the court to render a decision on the record.
