This court agrees with the submission of the respondent that the test for such bias is to be found in the dissenting opinion of deGranpré, J., in
Committee for Justice and Liberty Foun­dation et al. v. National Energy Board et al.
, [1978] 1 S.C.R. 369; 9 N.R. 115. This court must, therefore, look at whether there is a reasonable apprehension of bias. It must ask itself whether an in­formed person, viewing the matter realistically and practically - and having thought the matter through, would conclude that there was a reasonable apprehension of bias.
