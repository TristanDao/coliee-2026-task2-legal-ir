The second factor referred to in
Baker
, supra, is the nature of the statutory scheme and the terms of the statute pursuant to which a tribunal operates. Under the statutory scheme, and as exemplified by s. 92 of the
PSSRA
, supra, as cited above, the position of adjudicators is that of a final decision-maker in respect of a grievance by an employee. No appeal from an ad­judicator's decision is provided for, though, an employee may seek its judicial review. This suggests that more procedural safe­guards are appropriate.
