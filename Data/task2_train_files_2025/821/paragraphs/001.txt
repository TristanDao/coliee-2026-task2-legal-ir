Cullen, J.
: This is an application for judicial review brought pursuant to s. 18.1 of the
Federal Court Act
, S.C. 1990, c. 8, s. 5. The applicant seeks an order quashing a decision by the Public Service Staff Re­lations Board, dated October 20, 1998, denying a grievance by the applicant against his termination by Natural Resources Canada. The applicant also seeks an order finding in favour of his grievance or, in the alternative, an order requiring the Public Service Staff Relations Board to redetermine his grievance.
