The applicant submits that the ad­judicator erred in law when he denied the applicant's attempts to amend his pleadings during the hearing in an effort to claim additional relief. The applicant had not indicated the particular amount of damages which he was seeking in his original grievance form submitted to the Board. Subsequently, he had sought in his 349 page written submission to claim $200,000 in general damages and $10,000,000 in punitive damages. In rejecting these amendments, the adjudicator relied on
Burchill v. Attorney General of Canada
, [1981] 1 F.C. 109; 37 N.R. 530 (F.C.A.).
