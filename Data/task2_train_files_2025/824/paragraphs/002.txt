These three files involve judicial review applications seeking to set aside the decisions of an adjudicator at Passport Canada, issued on March 18, 2011, in which the adjudicator upheld the revocation of the applicants' passports and refused passport services to them for five years. Because the decisions rendered in the three files are nearly identical and the same circumstances gave rise to the three decisions, these reasons deal with all three applications for judicial review, and a copy of these reasons shall be placed in each file.
