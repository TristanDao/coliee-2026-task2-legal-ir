In exercise of that prerogative, the Governor in Council issued the Order, which governs the issuance, refusal and revocation of passports. The provisions of the Order pursuant to which the applicants' passports were revoked and further passport services were denied to them for five years are contained in paragraphs 9(a), 10(2)(b), 10(2)(c) and subsection 10(1) of the Order (and subsections 8(1) and 8(2) by reference), which provide as follows:
