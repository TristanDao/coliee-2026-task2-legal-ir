In October 2000, Mr. Elezi says the Socialist Party fraudulently won local elections. He says criminal groups helped the party, including one led by a man named Aldo Bare. Several months later, Mr. Elezi was fired from his job and replaced by a member of the Socialist Party. However, he continued to receive threats from people who blamed him for losing property that they had seized illegally in the first place during the communist regime.
