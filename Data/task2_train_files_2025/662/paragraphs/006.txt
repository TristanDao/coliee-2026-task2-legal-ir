To begin this analysis, I do not agree with the Applicants that the manner of questioning should be considered cumulatively with the other administrative problems experienced by them in the processing of their claims. There is no link, in my mind, between the scheduling difficulties and the manner of questioning in the hearing. Other than her illness on September 28, 2004 - which was not within her control - there is no evidence that this particular panel of the Board was the cause of the scheduling disruptions or that this panel acted in bad faith in the hearing process.
