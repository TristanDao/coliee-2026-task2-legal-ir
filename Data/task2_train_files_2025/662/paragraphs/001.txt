Snider, J.
: The Applicants are citizens of Colombia and comprise an extended family, all of whom base their refugee claim on a fear of persecution by the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia ("FARC"). The principal Applicant and his wife (Oscar and Maria Eugenia) are common-law spouses with three children. The eldest (Catalina) child is married to the Applicant son-in-law (Henry Mauricio). In a decision dated January 10, 2005, a panel of the Immigration and Refugee Board, Refugee Protection Division (the "Board") determined that the Applicants were neither Convention refugees nor persons in need of protection.
