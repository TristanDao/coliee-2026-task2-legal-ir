The Applicants submit that the cumulative impact of the many procedural problems and the manner of questioning is that the Board was in breach of its duty "to establish fair and efficient procedures that will maintain the integrity of the Canadian refugee protection system" (Guermache v. Canada(Minister of Citizenship and Immigration), [2004] F.C. 870, at para. 4). This is, in essence, an argument that the Board's behaviour amounts to an abuse of process which can, in certain circumstances, constitute an error even where the fairness of the hearing is not directly compromised.
