The Applicants assert that the Board committed a fatal error by not considering the risk to the Applicants based on the fact that the principal Applicant was the owner of a small business. The Applicants refer to a number of instances in the documentary evidence demonstrating that owners of small businesses are targeted for kidnapping by the FARC, even in Bogotá which was found, by the Board, to be an internal flight alternative ("IFA").
