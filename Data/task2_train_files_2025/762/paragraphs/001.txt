Phelan, J.
: The refusal to issue a Temporary Resident Permit (TRP) in generally unsympathetic circumstances must be quashed because of error in the decision. The Applicant has an extensive criminal record, questionable marital conduct and very little else to suggest that the equities are on his side. What he does have is a minor child in Canada whose existence and interest the Immigration Officer failed to consider.
