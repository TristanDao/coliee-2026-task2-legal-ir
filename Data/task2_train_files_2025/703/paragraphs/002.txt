The Applicant is a First Nation community in New Brunswick which has received approximately 14 million dollars annually in funding pursuant to an agreement with DIAND. In 2007, at the time of the impugned decision, the Applicant and DIAND were parties to a First Nations Funding Agreement for the term of April 1, 2005 to March 31, 2008 (the Agreement). It is pursuant to that Agreement that DIAND took the decision to appoint a third party manager.
