As for the Applicant's claims that it was unreasonable to rely on the AMDD report, the Respondent counters that it was reasonable to rely on the co-managers' opinion and its other experiences in dealing with similar situations. Also, it could not take into account any legal defences towards various creditors and the outstanding claim against the province until the issues were settled, the latter could not be considered as receivable and the claims from different creditors had to be considered as debts.
