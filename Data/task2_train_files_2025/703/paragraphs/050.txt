The Respondent submits that the Court should decline to exercise its discretion with respect to many of the remedies sought by the Applicant in the notice of application. The Respondents contends that quashing the decision would have unknown ramifications as it has been over two years since the decision was made and the evidence shows that, even today, the Applicant is in financial disarray. As for the declaration sought by the Applicant, it would similarly serve little purpose as it would leave the parties unclear as to what conduct they should adopt in light of the unknown changed circumstances.
