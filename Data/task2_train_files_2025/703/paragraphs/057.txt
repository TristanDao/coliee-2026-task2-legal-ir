The Applicant argues that the decision made by DIAND was unreasonable for a number of reasons including that it was unreasonable to rely on the information provided by the co-managers. However, I disagree with this and find that it was reasonable and necessary to rely on the report by AMDD. First of all, it is undisputed that the Applicant had been in default of the Agreement for numerous years for having an unacceptable cumulative deficit and had not fulfilled many of its reporting requirements.
