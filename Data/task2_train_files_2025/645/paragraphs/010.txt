The PRRA Officer emphasized that the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) had no knowledge of returning Tamils having been singled out for adverse treatment under Sri Lanka's Immigrants and Emigrants Act. Further, an immigration official at the Canadian High Commission in Colombo has stated that allegations that returnees to Sri Lanka are tortured are a complete fabrication. Returnees who do not have pending arrest warrants or active charges in Sri Lanka are simply released. Both the UNHCR and Canadian High Commission evidence revealed that some returnees may be questioned at the airport and then allowed to leave or otherwise not questioned at all.
