Since I find the evidence inadequate to found an aboriginal right pursuant to s. 35(1), and because argument was limited in regard to the relationship of ss. 25, 28 and 35 of the
Constitution Act, 1982
, it would be inappropriate to resolve the argument between the parties on the effect of that relationship. Interpretation of those sections in light of their interrelationship is left to be determined in a case that clearly raises the issue. In deferring resolution of that matter to another case, I follow the path of the Supreme Court of Canada in
Corbière
[see footnote 34].
