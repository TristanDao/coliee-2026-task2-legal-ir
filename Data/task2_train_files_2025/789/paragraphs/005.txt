With respect to the PIF of the second claimant who did not appear, the CRDD noted that neither his PIF nor the PIF of the first claimant included the normal covering page which warns claimants that their PIF could be used in other hearings. Therefore, the panel did not know if the second claimant had been warned of this. Notwithstanding, the panel accepted the second PIF into evidence, again redacting it so as to delete the same identifying information that had been removed from the PIF of the first claimant.
