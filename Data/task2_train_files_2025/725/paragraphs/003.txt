The Applicant's husband fled from Sri Lanka on December 3, 2001 to Singapore. The Applicant fled Sri Lanka to Singapore on March 3, 2002. With the help of an agent, they travelled together to the United States. Their intention was to reach Canada and claim refugee status. However, because they were relying upon false passports provided by the agent, they were intercepted at the airport in Chicago by US immigration authorities and detained. The Applicant was released in June 2002 as she was pregnant. She continued to Canada and claimed refugee status while her husband was still detained by US authorities.
