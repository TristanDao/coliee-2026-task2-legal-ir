The CAIPS notes in relation to the Applicant's application for her husband's permanent residence on March 31, 2005 show that the CHC wanted to obtain the Applicant's PIF and further evidence regarding her husband's detention by US Immigration. Instead of obtaining the PIF from their own records, or verifying with US authorities, they wrote to the Applicant's husband asking for this information on April 4, 2005. He responded by April 11, 2005.
