McKellar argues that the 30 day filing requirement for bringing an application for judicial review pursuant to section 18.1 (2) of the Federal Courts Act does not apply to a decision of the sort challenged here because it was not "a decision or an order of a federal board, commission or other tribunal". In that regard, it relies on the authority of Friedman & Friedman Inc. v. Canada (Superintendent of Bankruptcy) [2001] F.C.J. No. 124 and Coffey v. Canada (Minister of Justice), [2005] F.C.J. No. 689, 2005 FC 554.
