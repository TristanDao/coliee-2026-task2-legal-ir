I am also not unmindful that the problem of a missed filing deadline is curable by a motion for an extension that can be brought after the deadline has passed. This motion by the CRA, if allowed, would foreclose that opportunity by McKellar to seek an extension. In Vancouver Island Peace Society, above, at paragraph 24 Justice MacKay alluded to the possibility of a preliminary dismissal of an application where there existed a procedural irregularity which could not be corrected. The inference which I take from that remark is that a procedural irregularity which may be corrected is not sufficient to support the preliminary dismissal of an application.
