The Statement of Claim seeks a declara­tion that the plaintiffs have been members of the Ermineskin Indian Band No. 942 since November 16, 1937, or since their respective birth dates, if that date is later. The plaintiffs seek: a declaration that they are entitled to receive all benefits that have been received by members of the band; a declaration that they are entitled to receive future benefits made available to band members; an ac­counting of all benefits; and general damages plus interest. The claim alleges joint and several liability on the part of Her Majesty the Queen in Right of Canada as represented by the Minister of Indian and Inuit Affairs ("the Crown") and the Ermineskin defen­dants.
