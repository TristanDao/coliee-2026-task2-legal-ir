The Halifax Port Corporation's claim also seeks interest at the rate of 18% per annum from the invoice date of May 13, 1996, before and after the date of judgment. The amount of the claim and appropriate interest raises a statutory lien. In the absence of any claims by seamen for wages, that lien has first priority in any payment from the fund. It would be paid whenever the funds are paid, and regardless of the outcome of the appeal now open for leave to be heard by the Supreme Court of Canada, or further appeals in regard to the Judgment now is­sued. In these circumstancees, that Judgment directs payment of the amount invoiced, for services and fees in respect of the "Brussel", with interest at 18% per annum to July 24, 1996, and thereafter with interest at the rate attributed to the fund while held in the Court, up to the date of payment.
