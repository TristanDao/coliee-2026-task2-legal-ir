Reiter Petroleum also provided neces­saries to the ships "Deloris" and "Helen" at ports in the United States and it advances a claim against the "Brussel" as a sister ship. Both these ships were owned, along with the "Brussel", by Bulkcarriers and the three are sister ships. The claim for services to the sister ships raises a statutory in rem claim against the fund that ranks behind the claim of the mortgagee. Given the limits of the fund, Reiter's claims against the sister ships are not likely to be satisfied.
