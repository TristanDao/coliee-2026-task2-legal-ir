Patrick Stevedores No. 1 Ltd. carries on business in stevedoring and related marine cargo services at various ports in Australia. The outstanding debt claimed is AUS $1,591,739.22 for services to the "Brussel" and unspecified other vessels of the ABC fleet. No affidavit of foreign law was pro­vided, so the Court must apply the law of the forum to determine the status of this claim. Under Canadian maritime law, the provision of stevedoring and cargo services does not give rise to a claim in rem in pri­ority to the ship's mortgages. Therefore, the claim of Patrick Stevedores No. 1 Ltd. will not be satisfied from the fund.
