In an earlier case where the evidence did not clearly show the dates of breach or default, the Court ordered that conversion be made at the rate prevailing at the date of judgment. [see footnote 16] It is urged by counsel for Holt and other claimants that in the circumstances here, the appropriate date for conversion is that on which the amount of the claim is fixed, and payment out of Court is ordered. In my opinion, conversion at rates currently prevailing in February 2000 would significantly affect adversely the position of the mortgagee.
