Dixie Machine Welding and Metal Work, Inc. provided repair work to the "Helen" at Gulfport, Mississippi and now seeks to enforce its claim against the "Brussel". The claim of Dixie may create a maritime lien against the "Helen", but its claim against the "Brussel" constitutes a statutory in rem claim that ranks behind the mortgagee's interest. Given the size of the fund, Dixie's claim will not be satisfied.
