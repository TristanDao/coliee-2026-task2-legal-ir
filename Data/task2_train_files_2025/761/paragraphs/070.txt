Halterm is a Nova Scotia company that operates a marine container terminal, vessel berth and container storage facility. In Feb­ruary, 1995, Halterm entered into a contract for the provision of such services to ABC. Under the contract, Halterm provided these to the "Brussel" and to other ships owned by ABC and Bulkcarriers. [see footnote 24] Halterm claims that Deepsea Marine acted as the agent for the ship owners.
