Antwerp Shiprepair, based in Belgium, is claiming from the fund for unpaid amounts following the provision of ship repair services to the "Deloris" in September 1995. This claimant has not provided any evidence of foreign law. According to Cana­dian rules of conflicts of laws, when foreign law is unproven the Court must assume it is the same as the law of the forum. Under Canadian maritime law, ship repairers who are not in possession do not have priority ahead of the holder of the mortgages. For this reason, the claim of Antwerp Shiprepair N.V. will not be satisfied from the fund.
