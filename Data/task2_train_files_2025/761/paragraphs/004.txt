Messrs. T. Van Dooselare and F. De Roy, defendants in this matter, were ap­pointed trustees in Belgium to marshall and liquidate the assets of the two bankrupt companies. The trustees obtained an order in the Superior Court of Quebec in Bankruptcy in May 1996, which declared the Belgian bankruptcy order to be executory in Canada. The trustees sought to take possession of the "Brussel" as an asset of Bulkcarriers for liquidation and distribution among its credi­tors.
