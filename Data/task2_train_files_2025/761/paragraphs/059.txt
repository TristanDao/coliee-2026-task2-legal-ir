Turecamo Maritime Inc. provided dock­ing and other marine services to the "Brussel" at the port of Charleston, South Carolina on March 22 and 23, 1996. Under U.S. law, these are necessaries and thus, the amount outstanding, US $4,762.52, is se­cured by an American maritime lien. That sum, converted to Canadian currency, is payable from the fund, in priority to the ship's mortgages, with interest payable from April 1, 1996 at the contract rate of 1.5% per month to July 24, 1996 and thereafter at the rate for interest attributed to the fund.
