As for Halterm's sister ship claim, stemming from services provided to the "Antwerpen", it does constitute an in rem claim against the "Brussel". However, it does not rank in priority ahead of the ship's mortgages and it will not be satisfied from the fund. Further claims against the ships "Cornelis Verolme", "Ellen Hudig", and "Martha II" cannot be made against the proceeds of sale of the "Brussel", except as in personam claims against the owner and those would have no priority.
