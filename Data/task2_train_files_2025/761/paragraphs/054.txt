Ryan-Walsh Inc. provided stevedoring and other marine cargo services to the "Brussel" at the port of Charleston, South Carolina. The invoice for these services, for US $88,202.16, remains unpaid from the invoice date of March 29, 1996. Ryan-Walsh Inc. has an American maritime lien on the "Brussel" and the sum claimed, when con­verted to Canadian funds at the rate pre­vailing at the date of breach, is payable from the "Brussel" fund in priority to any pay­ment to the mortgagee. The payment to Ryan-Walsh shall include interest calculated at 7% per annum from the date of breach to the date of sale of the "Brussel", after which time interest will be calculated at the rate applicable to the accumulation of the fund.
