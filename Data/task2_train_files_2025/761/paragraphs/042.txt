The Atlantic Pilotage Authority also argues a claim against the fund for services provided to the "Antwerpen", a sister ship of the "Brussel". Under s. 43(8) of the
Federal Court Act
as applied in relation to claims in this case, the Authority has a statutory in rem claim against the "Brussel" for services provided to the "Antwerpen" in the sum of $1,547.20. This claim, however, ranks be­hind the ship's mortgages.
