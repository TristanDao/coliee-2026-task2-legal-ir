The South Carolina State Ports Au­thority claims against the proceeds of the sale of the "Brussel" for sums unpaid fol­lowing the provision of stevedoring and marine cargo services to the vessel at the port of Charleston, South Carolina. The total claimed is US $64,488.70, plus interest payable from the invoice date of April 1, 1996. [see footnote 23] The South Carolina State Ports Authority has an American mari­time lien on the "Brussel" and the sum claimed is payable, when converted to Cana­dian currency, from the proceeds of sale of the "Brussel" in priority to any payment to the mortgagee. Interest shall be in accord with the general principle here applied to all claims at the rate which the fund ac­cumulated after July 24, 1996.
