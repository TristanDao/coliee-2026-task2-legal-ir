An order goes which is intended to indicate ultimate disposal of the fund on the basis of the reasons here set out. It provides for payment now of the claims by Halifax Port Corporation and the Atlantic Pilotage Authority in relation to services rendered to the "Brussel". It also allows payment to the plaintiff, Holt, for counsel at solicitor/client rates, for reasonable expenses incurred in the appraisal and sale of the "Brussel", upon presentation of a bill of costs.
