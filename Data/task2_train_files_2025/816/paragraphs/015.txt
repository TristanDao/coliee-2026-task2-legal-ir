The respondent points to the decision of the Federal Court of Appeal in
Ramirez v. Minister of Employment and Immigration
, [1992] 2 F.C. 306; 135 N.R. 390 (F.C.A.), where the court set out certain principles to help determine when a claimant should be excluded under Article 1F(a). The respondent's argument appears to be that if the Board's decision is made in accordance with these principles, then the decision is proper.
