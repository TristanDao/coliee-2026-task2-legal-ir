By virtue of subsection 87(2) of the
Exchequer Court Act
, the judges were permitted to make rules empowering the Registrar or Master to exercise any authority or jurisdiction of a judge. The
General Rules and Orders
of the Exchequer Court outlined the jurisdiction to be exercised by the Registrar or Master and provided for an appeal to the Court, which was defined as meaning a judge of the Exchequer Court.
