The
Federal Court Rules
, 1998 are subordinate legislation and cannot prevail over any provision in an Act of Parliament or a regulation. In that regard, Rule 1(2) provides as follows:
"1(2) In the event of any inconsistency between these Rules and an Act of Parlia­ment or a regulation made thereunder, that Act or regulation prevails to the extent of the inconsistency."
"1(2) Les dispositions de toute loi fédérale ou de ses textes d'application l'emportent sur les dispositions incompatibles des présentes règles."
