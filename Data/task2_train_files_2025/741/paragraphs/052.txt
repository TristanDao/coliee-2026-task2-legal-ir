As indicated earlier in these reasons, following the opportunity to make oral representations that was presided over by the Minister of State, the Minister of State reported to the Minister of Finance and included in that report conclusions and recommendations. That the Minister of State would be reporting on the representations he received was made abundantly clear, according to the transcript of the meeting, at both the beginning of the meeting and at the close of the meeting. However, in neither statement did the Minister of State indicate that he would be drawing conclusions and making recommendations.
