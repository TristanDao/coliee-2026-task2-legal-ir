The report of the Minister of State contained no new facts. Counsel for Sovereign argued that it was not a fair and complete summary of a hearing of Sovereign's representatives. Therefore, there was not before the Minister of Finance for his consideration in reaching his decision their representations or a fair and reasonable summary thereof. I reject that argument. There is no evidence before me on which to base a conclusion that Sovereign's written representations, and the transcript of its representatives' oral representations were not before the Minister.
