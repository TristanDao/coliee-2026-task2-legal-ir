Evaluating credibility and weighing evidence falls within the jurisdiction of the tribunal assessing a refugee protection claimant's allegation of a subjective fear (
Cepeda-Gutierrez v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration
) (1998), 157 F.T.R. 35, 83 A.C.W.S. (3d) 264 at paragraph 14). Prior to
Dunsmuir v. New Brunswick
, 2008 SCC 9, [2008] 1 S.C.R. 190, the applicable standard of review under such circumstances was patent unreasonableness; it is now reasonableness.
