In 1993, the Applicant and his brother purchased land together in Vietnam. Over time the land increased in value. In late 2000, it is alleged that the local Hai Phong government sought to expropriate the land from the Applicant, his brother, and 21 other families residing in the area without providing adequate compensation. The Applicant participated in a petition against the expropriation. The habitants were threatened with jail or death if they did not agree to the government plans. The expropriation was complete by early 2001.
