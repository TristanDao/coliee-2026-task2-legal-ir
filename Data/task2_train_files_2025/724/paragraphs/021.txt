The social services here at issue were identified as special education and training, sheltered employment and respite care. The officer acknowledged on cross examination that respite care would probably not be needed. [see footnote 6] Further, the officer acknowledged that she did not see the response provided by the applicant to her "fairness letter" [see footnote 7]. At the same time, the officer acknowledged that she had an obligation to assure herself that the medical notification that was before her was reasonable, on the basis of the material [see footnote 8], and I am satisfied that the reference to "the material" extends to
all
relevant material which, on the facts of this matter, certainly extends to the response to the "fairness letter".
