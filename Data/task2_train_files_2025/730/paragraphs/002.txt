This action was begun by Statement of Claim, filed on July 30, 1986. The plaintiffs claimed damages that were alleged to have arisen when the defendants breached their contract(s) to tow the plaintiffs' vessel into the harbour at St. John's, Newfoundland by failing to complete the operation. Defences were filed in response to the Statement of Claim on September 26 and October 2, 1986 in which the defendants denied the existence of any such contract.
