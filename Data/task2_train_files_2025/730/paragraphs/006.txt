Between April 15, 1992 and March 10, 1993, there was no communication from the plaintiffs' solicitor. On April 7, 1993, the solicitor for the defendants reaffirmed his requirement for the payment of security for costs. In September 1993, the plaintiffs' solicitor received instructions from his clients to agree to the amount requested.
