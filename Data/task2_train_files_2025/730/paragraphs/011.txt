In my opinion, there is little doubt that the delays caused by the plaintiffs' inaction, particularly that spanning the period between the filing of their Statement of Claim in 1986 and the delivery of their List of Documents in 1991, can be characterized as inordinate. The cause of action arose in 1984, some ten (10) years ago, the limitation period expired in 1990 and as yet there has been comparatively little progress in bringing the case on for trial. In addition, the plaintiffs have not satisfied me that the delay caused by them was excusable. Therefore, the resolution of this motion will turn on whether the defendants' right to a fair trial has been prejudiced as a consequence of this delay.
