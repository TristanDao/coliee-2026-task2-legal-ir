The validity of the direction to report, as addressed to the adult applicant and her two sons, in light of the valid deportation order issued to the adult claimant only, was not argued in any depth in the hearing of this matter. In my view, whether the sons are properly included in the removal process is a matter that the respondent should carefully consider. If the children are permitted to remain in Canada, whether they do so or return to Pakistan with their mother should she be removed, will be a matter for the family to determine.
