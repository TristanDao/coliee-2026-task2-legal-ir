On Friday, September 10, 1993, an application for leave and for judicial review was filed in the court on behalf of the applicants seeking an extension of time and leave of the court to commence an application for judicial review in relation to the decision made by the first level tribunal on September 4, 1991. Submissions concerning the application for an extension of time and concerning the application for leave to commence proceedings were obviously not complete when, on the same day, counsel on behalf of the applicants filed the notice of motion now under consideration for a stay of execution of the removal order.
