On August 31, 1993 Mrs. Naseem was served with a direction to report at Pearson International Airport on September 16, 1993, to be removed from Canada to Pakistan. The direction to report is evidently a follow-up to the deportation order made against and issued to Mrs. Naseem on September 4, 1991. That deportation order relates only to the adult applicant, Nasreen Naseem. It is averred by affidavit of the husband and father that a term originally typed in that order, which specified "also included on this order are Fraz Syed and Faisal Syed Nasreen" (the two children of the family), was struck out by the adjudicator before the order was issued. The direction to report, for removal from Canada on September 16, 1993 is directed to "Mrs. Nasreen Naseem and children".
