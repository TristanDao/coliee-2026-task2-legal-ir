The adult applicant and her children are dependant upon her husband and their father, also a citizen of Pakistan who claimed Convention refugee status after his arrival in Canada. His claim was separately considered and it was determined that he did not have a credible basis to his claim to refugee status, by decision of a first level panel on December 4, 1992. Thereafter, the husband Syed Mohammed Nasim applied for leave to commence proceedings under s. 18.1 of the
Federal Court Act
to review and set aside the decision of the tribunal and to set aside the departure notice issued to him by the adjudicator on the day the tribunal's decision was made. That application for leave has not been determined and is still pending before the court.
