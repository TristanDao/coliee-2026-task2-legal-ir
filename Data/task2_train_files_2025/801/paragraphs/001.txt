MacKay, J.
: These are summary reasons for an order made at the conclusion of a telephone conference hearing of the applicant's motion for an order effectively granting a stay of execution of a removal order directed against the applicant, or in the alternative granting the applicant's motion for an extension of time to commence an application for leave and for judicial review. The application for leave, if the extension of time be granted, seeks leave and judicial review in regard to the decision of the Convention Refugee Determination Division (the "C.R.D.D."), at Calgary, Alberta, on April 8, 1994, determining that the applicant is not a Convention refugee.
