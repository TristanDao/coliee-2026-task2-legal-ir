After hearing counsel for the applicant, from Calgary, and for the respondent, from Edmonton, by telephone on May 12, 1994, this court ordered as follows:
"IT IS ORDERED that the application for leave and for judicial review and for an extension of time, filed on behalf of the applicant on May 10, 1994 is an application before the court and is deemed to be an application for leave within paragraph 49(1)(c)(i) of the
Immigration Act
, and a stay of execution of the removal order relating to the applicant is operative by that paragraph.
"IT IS FURTHER ORDERED and hereby certified, pursuant to s. 83(1) of the
Immigration Act
that the following is a serious question of general importance:
'Where an application for leave and for judicial review pursuant to s. 82.1 of the
Immigration Act
, including an application for an extension of time to file the application, is filed, beyond the time limited by s. 82.1(3), but has not been considered by the court, does paragraph 49(1)(c)(i) apply to preclude execution of a removal order pending disposition of the application by the court?'"
