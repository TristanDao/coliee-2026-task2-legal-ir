Conversely, in Hu v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration), [2003] F.C.J. No. 788 (T.D.)(QL), O'Keefe J. was unwilling to deny the application for judicial review because he could not discern "how much influence the use of [evidence from a separate hearing] had on the Board's ultimate credibility findings.
