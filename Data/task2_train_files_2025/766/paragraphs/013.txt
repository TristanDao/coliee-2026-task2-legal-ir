The Climate Change Plan sets new emission reduction targets well above Canada's Kyoto commitments based on a series of proposed regulatory changes, new conservation programs, research and development initiatives, incentives and collaborative action. All of these measures are projected to reduce Canada's average annual greenhouse gas emissions between 2008 and 2012 to 755 Mt CO
2
equivalent - a figure that is 34% higher than Canada's Kyoto target for those years.
