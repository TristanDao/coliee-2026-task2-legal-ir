Following its introduction to Parliament as a private member's bill (Bill C-288), the
KPIA
became law on June 22, 2007. The
KPIA
was not supported by the government which had earlier stated that Canada would not comply with the Kyoto Protocol targets. The
KPIA
thus embodies a legislative policy which is inconsistent with stated government policy. This also explains why the
KPIA
does not authorize the expenditure of public funds to achieve its objectives. A money bill cannot be introduced to Parliament unless it is presented by the government.
