With respect to Jager, I am satisfied that Jager can be considered an expert at least in the vocabulary, terminology and terms of art used in the OSB industry in North America. I am also satisfied that he is an expert "with regard to the operation and functioning of this type of equipment, including the feed system for flakers for the Oriented Strand Board Industry in North America". Therefore, I consider Jager to be an expert with respect to the subject matter of the patent in issue.
