According to the first McDonald affidavit, the first two batch feeding systems that were put into operation are the CAE batch feeding system which was installed in the Normick Chambord plant in Chambord, Quebec in 1988 and the PS & E batch feeding system installed in the Northwood plant in Bemidji, Minnesota in 1988. McDonald was involved in the design and manufacture of the CAE Feeding System in Chambord.
