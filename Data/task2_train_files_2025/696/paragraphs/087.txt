Although in the detailed description of the preferred embodiments it is acknowledged that the lumber may be deposited from the hopper to the feed compartment in more than one way, the best method is still considered to be by free fall. Page 6 of the patent reads as follows:
"... with regard first to the process,
the first process step is the loading of the feed compartment and is carried out from above, preferably by free fall in the form of a compact axially aligned lumber pile
. Owing to the mutual support of the pieces of lumber during the loading operation, there is no possibility whatsoever of there changing their mutual positions and thus being laid at an angle in the feed compartment. They therefore retain their axial alignment, adopted in the proceeding compacting of the lumber pile with some assistance of the operating personnel." (Emphasis added)
