As I indicated earlier in my reasons, given my findings on the issues, I would allow the defendants motions for summary judgment. Rule 432.3(4) provides that where there is a genuine issue for trial the judge may nevertheless grant summary judgment in favour of either party, either upon issue or generally, unless the judge is unable on the whole of the evidence to find the facts necessary to decide the questions of fact or law or the judge considers that it would be unjust to decide the issues on the motions for summary judgment.
