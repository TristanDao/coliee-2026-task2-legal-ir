In the OSB Industry there are two basic types of flakers. First there are flakers that are cylindrical in shape and use multiple knives mounted on the inside of the cylinder. The flaking compartment into which the logs are fed, itself inside the cylinder, is known as the ring flaker. Second, there are circular disc flakers which have multiple knives mounted radially on the face of the disc. The flaking compartment into which the logs are fed, is located in front of the disc. The disc travels through the logs in the flaking process. This is referred to as a disc flaker.
