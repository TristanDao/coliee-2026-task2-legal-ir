On March 17, 2010, Prothonotary Lafrenière dismissed AIMCan's motion for leave of the Court to allow its officers to represent the corporation without counsel. Prothonotary Lafrenière's Order was appealed, and Justice Gauthier dismissed the appeal on April 30, 2010. AIMCan was not represented in this application and other than filing a Notice of Appearance, it has not filed any responding materials.
