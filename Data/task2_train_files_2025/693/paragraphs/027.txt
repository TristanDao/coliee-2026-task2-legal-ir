I reject the respondent's argument that an extension would inflict prejudice upon her. The inconvenience she mentions does not follow from the delay but from the very fact that her grant of citizenship has been challenged. The same prejudice would have arisen even if the Minister had filed this application the day after the Citizenship Judge rendered the decision under review. The application was filed six weeks after the decision was rendered and two weeks late. This delay is not so great as to inflict any prejudice which would not have existed otherwise.
