I must also agree with the Respondent that the appropriate decision-maker in this instance is the DCBA as opposed to the CDS. The responsibility for administrative decision-making related to relocation benefits is delegated by way of section 14.1.02 of the Relocation Direction, APS 2008. The DCBA can approve a request to exceed the three-year period after the release date in "exceptional circumstances." This decision has yet to be raised directly with or made by the DCBA.
