The Applicants and the Respondents each filed an affidavit from an expert witness. The Respondents engaged Professor Sara Horowitz, a Professor of Comparative Literature at York University with specialties in the Holocaust and Jewish studies. Her affidavit focuses on the use of various themes and elements in Holocaust literature; she deposes that various elements in the Book, such as a massacre at a factory, aktionens or raids in Jewish ghettoes, and hiding in attics and lofts, are common themes in Holocaust literature.
