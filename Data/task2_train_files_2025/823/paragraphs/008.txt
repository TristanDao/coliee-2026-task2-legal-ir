The parties agree there was an offer from Ms. Witterick to Ms. Maltz to include "something" from Ms. Maltz in the Book. Ms. Maltz characterizes this as Ms. Witterick looking for an endorsement; Ms. Witterick characterizes this as offering Ms. Maltz an opportunity to include her views in the Book, after Ms. Witterick learnt that Ms. Maltz was upset by the Documentary not being mentioned in the Book. In any event, there is no mention of the Documentary in the copies of the Book placed in the record for this matter.
