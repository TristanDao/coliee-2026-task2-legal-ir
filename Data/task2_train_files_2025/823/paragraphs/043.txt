As to the extent to which the alleged infringement has adversely affected and diminished the value of the Applicants' copyright, the Applicants allege that the Book is being mistaken as the true story, that the Documentary is now used less for learning and education in favour of the Book, that they are receiving less speaking engagements, and that the number of screenings of the Documentary has declined. However, the evidence in the record shows that the number of speaking engagements and screenings were declining even before publication of the Book, a fact which casts some doubt on the cause of this decline.
