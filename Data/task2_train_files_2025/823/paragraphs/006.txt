Ms. Maltz says she first learned of Ms. Witterick's Book on April 2, 2013, when she received an email from a woman named Sue Kaplan. On April 3, 2013, she contacted Ms. Witterick by email and, following their exchange of emails over the next two days, Ms. Witterick sent Ms. Maltz several copies of the Book. Ms. Maltz says she was distressed by the situation and did not read the Book until sometime after she received the copies.
