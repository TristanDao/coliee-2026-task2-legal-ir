The Applicants seek $2,000,000 in damages for the alleged infringement of their moral rights by the Respondents. They claim that their rights to be associated with the Book and to the integrity of the Documentary have each been infringed. Yet, the Applicants cite no case law in support of their claims of moral rights infringement. Indeed, the Applicants' Memorandum of Fact and Law scarcely mentions their claims in this regard and moral rights were hardly mentioned at all at the hearing of this matter. Furthermore, and in any event, the Applicants could not reasonably expect that the Book would attribute anything at all to the Documentary because Ms. Maltz did not pursue Ms. Witterick's offer to include "something" about the Documentary in the Book.
