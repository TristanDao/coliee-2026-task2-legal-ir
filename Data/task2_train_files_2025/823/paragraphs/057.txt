THIS COURT'S JUDGMENT is that
:
1. The Applicants' application pursuant to subsection 34(4) of the
Copyright Act
is dismissed; and
2. The Respondents shall have their costs of this application in such amount as may be agreed to by the parties. If the parties are unable to agree as to the amount of such costs within 15 days of the date of this judgment, either party shall thereafter be at liberty to apply for an assessment of costs in accordance with the
Federal Courts Rules
.
