The Respondents recognize that the Applicants used their skill and judgment to put together and edit archival material, film footage, music, and conduct interviews for the Documentary; these elements give the Documentary a "distinct feel" involving contemporary recollection and history. The Book, however, has a completely different feel, the Respondents say, because it is a fictional story aimed at young readers. The Respondents argue that the Applicants have failed to consider the Book as a whole, including what makes the Book an original work.
