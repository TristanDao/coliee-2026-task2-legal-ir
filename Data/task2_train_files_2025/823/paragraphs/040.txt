The Applicants claim infringement by the Respondents in respect of the well-delineated characters in the Documentary, and rely on
Anne of Green Gables
, arguing that the characters in the Book are clearly based on and are virtually identical to the individuals in the Documentary. The Applicants' reliance upon
Anne of Green Gables
is misguided, however, because there are no fictional characters in the Documentary; there are only real people or references to and recollections of once real persons, and there cannot be copyright over a real person, whether dead or alive.
