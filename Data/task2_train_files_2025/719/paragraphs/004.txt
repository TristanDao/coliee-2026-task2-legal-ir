The Minister confirmed the reassessments on June 19, 1995. Metro-Can appealed to the Tax Court of Canada; the appeal was dismissed on October 2, 1998. Metro-Can appealed that decision to the Federal Court of Appeal, which dismissed the appeal on June 22, 2000 [see 259 N.R. 206]. Subsequently, the Supreme Court of Canada dismissed Metro-Can's application for leave to appeal on March 15, 2001 [see 268 N.R. 395]. Finally, on June 28, 2001, the Federal Court of Appeal dismissed Metro-Can's application to re-hear the case [see 273 N.R. 273].
